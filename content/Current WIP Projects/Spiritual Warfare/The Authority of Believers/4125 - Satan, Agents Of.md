Satan works in this world through people and through those spiritual beings who acknowledge his authority. His agents oppose God's will and purposes.

Satan works through people

Satan's human agents pursue his evil desires

[John 8:44](https://biblehub.com/john/8-44.htm) Jesus Christ speaking of the relationship of some unbelieving Jews to Satan. _See also_ [Acts 13:10](https://biblehub.com/acts/13-10.htm); [1 John 3:8-10](https://biblehub.com/1_john/3-8.htm)

Satan works through people to oppose God's purposes

[Acts 5:1-9](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-1.htm) Ananias and Sapphira; [Matthew 16:23](https://biblehub.com/matthew/16-23.htm) pp [Mark 8:33](https://biblehub.com/mark/8-33.htm) Simon Peter

Judas Iscariot:

[Luke 22:3](https://biblehub.com/luke/22-3.htm); [John 6:70](https://biblehub.com/john/6-70.htm); [John 13:2](https://biblehub.com/john/13-2.htm) [Acts 13:8-10](https://biblehub.com/acts/13-8.htm) Elymas, the sorcerer

Examples of people doing Satan's work

False prophets

[Deuteronomy 13:5](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/13-5.htm); [Jeremiah 23:26-27](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/23-26.htm); [Jeremiah 28:15](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/28-15.htm); [Jeremiah 29:21](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/29-21.htm); [Ezekiel 13:6-9](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/13-6.htm); [Matthew 7:15](https://biblehub.com/matthew/7-15.htm); [Matthew 24:24](https://biblehub.com/matthew/24-24.htm); [2 Peter 2:1-3](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/2-1.htm)

Others who lead people away from God

[Deuteronomy 13:13](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/13-13.htm); [2 Kings 21:9-26](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/21-9.htm) Manasseh and his son, Amon; [Proverbs 7:21](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/7-21.htm) the adulteress; [Isaiah 3:12](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/3-12.htm); [Jeremiah 50:6](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/50-6.htm); [2 Peter 2:18-19](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/2-18.htm)

Satan works through his spiritual agents

Satan's angels

[Matthew 25:41](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-41.htm) _See also_ [2 Peter 2:4](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/2-4.htm); [Revelation 12:7](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-7.htm) "angel" and "messenger" are the same word in Greek

Satan's demons

[Luke 13:11-16](https://biblehub.com/luke/13-11.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 12:22](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-22.htm); [Luke 11:14-15](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-14.htm)

Satan's agents are identified by various images and titles

The little horn

[Daniel 8:9-12](https://biblehub.com/daniel/8-9.htm) This probably represents Antiochus Epiphanes.

The prince of Persia

[Daniel 10:13](https://biblehub.com/daniel/10-13.htm) probably representing a demon with influence over the Persian kingdom

The king of the North

[Daniel 11:28](https://biblehub.com/daniel/11-28.htm) probably representing Antiochus Epiphanes

The antichrist

[1 John 4:1-4](https://biblehub.com/1_john/4-1.htm); 2Jn 7

The man of lawlessness

[2 Thessalonians 2:3-10](https://biblehub.com/2_thessalonians/2-3.htm)

The beast

[Revelation 11:7](https://biblehub.com/revelation/11-7.htm); [Revelation 13:1-8](https://biblehub.com/revelation/13-1.htm)-18; [Revelation 14:9-11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/14-9.htm); [Revelation 16:13](https://biblehub.com/revelation/16-13.htm); [Revelation 17:8](https://biblehub.com/revelation/17-8.htm); [Revelation 19:20](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-20.htm); [Revelation 20:10](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-10.htm)

The false prophet

[Revelation 16:13](https://biblehub.com/revelation/16-13.htm); [Revelation 19:20](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-20.htm)

The prostitute or Babylon the Great

[Revelation 14:8](https://biblehub.com/revelation/14-8.htm); [Revelation 16:19](https://biblehub.com/revelation/16-19.htm); [Revelation 17:1-18](https://biblehub.com/revelation/17-1.htm); [Revelation 18:1-10](https://biblehub.com/revelation/18-1.htm); [Revelation 19:2](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-2.htm)
