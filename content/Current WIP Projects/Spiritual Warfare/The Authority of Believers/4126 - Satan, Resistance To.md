Jesus Christ resisted Satan in his ministry, and believers are commanded to follow his example in this respect. God assists believers in this struggle.

**Satan can be resisted**

His power is limited

[Job 1:12](https://biblehub.com/job/1-12.htm); [Job 2:6](https://biblehub.com/job/2-6.htm); [1 Corinthians 10:13](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/10-13.htm); [Revelation 12:12](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-12.htm); [Revelation 20:3](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-3.htm)

Jesus Christ resists Satan

[Matthew 4:1-11](https://biblehub.com/matthew/4-1.htm) pp [Luke 4:1-13](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-1.htm); [John 1:5](https://biblehub.com/john/1-5.htm); [Hebrews 2:18](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/2-18.htm); [Hebrews 4:15](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/4-15.htm)

**Christians can resist Satan**

[James 4:7](https://biblehub.com/james/4-7.htm) _See also_ [Luke 10:19](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-19.htm); [Romans 6:13](https://biblehub.com/romans/6-13.htm); [Ephesians 4:26-27](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/4-26.htm); [Ephesians 6:11-13](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/6-11.htm); [1 Peter 5:8-9](https://biblehub.com/1_peter/5-8.htm); [2 Peter 3:17](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/3-17.htm); [1 John 2:13-14](https://biblehub.com/1_john/2-13.htm)

God protects his people from Satan

[2 Thessalonians 3:3](https://biblehub.com/2_thessalonians/3-3.htm) _See also_ [1 John 5:18](https://biblehub.com/1_john/5-18.htm)

God's protection is to be sought in prayer

[John 17:15](https://biblehub.com/john/17-15.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 6:13](https://biblehub.com/matthew/6-13.htm); [Luke 22:31-32](https://biblehub.com/luke/22-31.htm); [Ephesians 6:11](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/6-11.htm)

**Jesus Christ has the victory over Satan**

Jesus Christ's defeat of Satan on the cross

[Colossians 2:15](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-15.htm) _See also_ [John 12:31](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm); [Revelation 12:11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-11.htm)

[Philippians 2:10](https://biblehub.com/philippians/2-10.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 45:23](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/45-23.htm); [Psalm 118:10-12](https://biblehub.com/psalms/118-10.htm); [Mark 16:17-18](https://biblehub.com/mark/16-17.htm); [Luke 10:17](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-17.htm); [Acts 16:18](https://biblehub.com/acts/16-18.htm)
