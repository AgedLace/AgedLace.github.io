[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [4000 Creation](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4000.htm)  "  [4100 Supernatural beings](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4100.htm)  "  [4165 exorcism](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4165.htm)

The expulsion of an evil spirit or spirits from a person. Jesus Christ has unique authority as an exorcist. He delegated this authority to the disciples.

Exorcism was practised with varying degrees of success by Jews and pagans

[Matthew 7:22](https://biblehub.com/matthew/7-22.htm); [Matthew 12:27](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-27.htm) pp [Luke 11:19](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-19.htm); [Acts 19:13-16](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-13.htm)

Jesus Christ had unique authority as an exorcist

[Mark 1:27](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-27.htm) pp [Luke 4:36](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-36.htm) [Matthew 12:25-28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-25.htm) pp [Luke 11:17-18](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-17.htm) The authority Jesus Christ exercised over evil spirits demonstrated that God's kingly authority was present in him. _See also_ [Matthew 12:29](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-29.htm) pp [Mark 3:27](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-27.htm) pp [Luke 11:21-22](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-21.htm) Jesus Christ is the "strong man". His power over evil spirits shows that Satan is defeated.

Jesus Christ had a general ministry of exorcism

[Matthew 8:16](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-16.htm) pp [Mark 1:34](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-34.htm) pp [Luke 4:41](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-41.htm); [Mark 1:39](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-39.htm); [Luke 6:17-18](https://biblehub.com/luke/6-17.htm); [Luke 7:21](https://biblehub.com/luke/7-21.htm)

Jesus Christ gave the apostles power to cast out evil spirits

[Matthew 10:1](https://biblehub.com/matthew/10-1.htm) pp [Mark 6:7](https://biblehub.com/mark/6-7.htm) pp [Luke 9:1](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-1.htm); [Mark 3:14-15](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-14.htm); [Mark 16:17](https://biblehub.com/mark/16-17.htm); [Luke 10:17](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-17.htm)

After the ascension the apostles cast out evil spirits

[Acts 5:16](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-16.htm); [Acts 19:12](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-12.htm)

Various symptoms associated with the need for exorcism

Violent or self-destructive behaviour

[Mark 5:1-5](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-1.htm) pp [Luke 8:27-29](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-27.htm)

Deafness and muteness

[Matthew 9:32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-32.htm); [Mark 9:17](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-17.htm); [Luke 11:14](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-14.htm)

Seizures

[Matthew 17:14-15](https://biblehub.com/matthew/17-14.htm) pp [Mark 9:17-18](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-17.htm) pp [Luke 9:38-39](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-38.htm)

Various components of exorcism

A word of command

[Mark 1:25](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-25.htm) pp [Luke 4:35](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-35.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 8:32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-32.htm); [Mark 9:25](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-25.htm); [Acts 16:18](https://biblehub.com/acts/16-18.htm)

The demons recognise the source of the authority addressing them

[Mark 1:24](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-24.htm) pp [Luke 4:34](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-34.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 8:29](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-29.htm) pp [Mark 5:7](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-7.htm) pp [Luke 8:28](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-28.htm); [Acts 16:17](https://biblehub.com/acts/16-17.htm)

Exorcism was often accompanied by an element of violence or trauma

[Mark 1:26](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-26.htm) _See also_ [Mark 9:26](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-26.htm); [Matthew 8:32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-32.htm) pp [Mark 5:13](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-13.htm) pp [Luke 8:32-33](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-32.htm); [Acts 19:16](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-16.htm)

Exorcism changes the behaviour or condition of the person delivered

[Mark 5:15](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-15.htm) pp [Luke 8:35](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-35.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 9:33](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-33.htm) pp [Luke 11:14](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-14.htm)

An exorcised person may be "repossessed"

[Matthew 12:43-45](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-43.htm) pp [Luke 11:24-26](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-24.htm)

Methods of exorcism other than the word of command

Exorcism may be achieved at a distance

[Matthew 15:21-28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/15-21.htm) pp [Mark 7:24-30](https://biblehub.com/mark/7-24.htm); [Acts 19:12](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-12.htm)

On rare occasions an object is used

[Acts 19:12](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-12.htm) This was an unusual demonstration of power in a city noted for magic.
