## 5544 soldiers

[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [5000 Humanity](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5000.htm)  "  [5200 Human civilisation](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5200.htm)  "  [5544 soldiers](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5544.htm)

Active members of an army, whatever their rank. Paul uses the term frequently in his letters to draw attention to the fact that believers are engaged in spiritual warfare.
