#NT/Revelation-12v11  

> [!NOTE] **Revelation 12:11**
> 
> They have conquered him by the blood of the Lamb and by the word of their testimony. And they did not love their lives so as to shy away from death.

- *conquered - G3528 - nikao* #Greek/G3528-nikao  
	- **Strong's Concordance**
		- conquer, prevail
		- victorious, overcome, prevail, subdue
	- **HELPS Word-studies**
		- **3528** _nikáō_(from [3529](https://biblehub.com/greek/3529.htm) _/níkē_, "victory") – properly, _conquer_ (overcome); " 'to carry off the victory, come off victorious.' The verb implies a battle" (K. Wuest).
	- **Strong's Exhaustive Concordance**
		- conquer, overcome, prevail
		- From [nike](https://biblehub.com/greek/3529.htm); to subdue (literally or figuratively) -- conquer, overcome, prevail, get the victory.
