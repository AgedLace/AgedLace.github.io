God frequently rescues people from danger, suffering and sin, often through human agency.

[Psalm 3:8](https://biblehub.com/psalms/3-8.htm) _See also_ [Exodus 3:7-8](https://biblehub.com/exodus/3-7.htm); [Psalm 18:2](https://biblehub.com/psalms/18-2.htm); [Daniel 3:16-17](https://biblehub.com/daniel/3-16.htm); [Jonah 2:9](https://biblehub.com/jonah/2-9.htm)
