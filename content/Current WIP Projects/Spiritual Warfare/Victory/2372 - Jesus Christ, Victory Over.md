Jesus Christ triumphs over and disarms all the powers of evil arrayed against him. Believers are able to share in this victory through faith in Jesus Christ.

The scope of Jesus Christ's victory

Over temptation

[Hebrews 4:15](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/4-15.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 4:1-11](https://biblehub.com/matthew/4-1.htm) pp [Luke 4:1-13](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-1.htm)

Over sin

[Romans 5:20-21](https://biblehub.com/romans/5-20.htm) _See also_ [1 John 3:5](https://biblehub.com/1_john/3-5.htm)

Over Satan

[John 12:31](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm) Satan is called "the prince of this world" in John's Gospel. _See also_ [Luke 10:18-19](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-18.htm); [John 14:30](https://biblehub.com/john/14-30.htm); [John 16:11](https://biblehub.com/john/16-11.htm); [Hebrews 2:14-15](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/2-14.htm)

Over evil spirits

[Mark 1:23-27](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-23.htm) pp [Luke 4:33-36](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-33.htm)

Over all powers

[Colossians 2:15](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-15.htm) "powers and authorities" include evil spiritual beings. _See also_ [Romans 8:38-39](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-38.htm); [1 Corinthians 15:24-25](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/15-24.htm); [Ephesians 1:21-22](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/1-21.htm); [Ephesians 4:8](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/4-8.htm); [Psalm 68:18](https://biblehub.com/psalms/68-18.htm)

Over the world

[John 16:33](https://biblehub.com/john/16-33.htm) Here, as elsewhere in John's Gospel, "world" refers to the human race in rebellion against God. _See also_ [2 Thessalonians 2:8](https://biblehub.com/2_thessalonians/2-8.htm) "the lawless one" represents the leader of human forces of evil; [1 John 5:4-5](https://biblehub.com/1_john/5-4.htm); [Revelation 17:12-14](https://biblehub.com/revelation/17-12.htm); [Revelation 19:11-21](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-11.htm)

Over death

[Romans 6:9](https://biblehub.com/romans/6-9.htm) _See also_ [Acts 2:24](https://biblehub.com/acts/2-24.htm); [1 Corinthians 15:26](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/15-26.htm)-57; [2 Timothy 1:10](https://biblehub.com/2_timothy/1-10.htm); [Revelation 1:18](https://biblehub.com/revelation/1-18.htm)

The manner of Jesus Christ's victory

By the written word of God

[Matthew 4:4-10](https://biblehub.com/matthew/4-4.htm) pp [Luke 4:4-12](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-4.htm)

By his death on the cross

[Hebrews 2:14](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/2-14.htm) _See also_ [Colossians 2:15](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-15.htm); [Revelation 12:11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-11.htm)

By God's authority

[Luke 11:20](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-20.htm) pp [Matthew 12:28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-28.htm)

By his spoken word

[Matthew 8:16](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-16.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 8:8](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-8.htm) pp [Luke 7:7](https://biblehub.com/luke/7-7.htm); [Matthew 8:13](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-13.htm)

Consequences of Jesus Christ's victory

Assurance for believers

[John 16:33](https://biblehub.com/john/16-33.htm); [1 Thessalonians 4:13](https://biblehub.com/1_thessalonians/4-13.htm); [Revelation 5:5](https://biblehub.com/revelation/5-5.htm)

Victory for believers

[1 Corinthians 15:57](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/15-57.htm) _See also_ [Romans 7:21-25](https://biblehub.com/romans/7-21.htm); [Romans 8:37](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-37.htm); [Romans 16:20](https://biblehub.com/romans/16-20.htm)
