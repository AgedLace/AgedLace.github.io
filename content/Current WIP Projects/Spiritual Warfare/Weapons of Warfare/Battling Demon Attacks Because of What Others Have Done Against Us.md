**SOURCE** - [3rd and 4th Levels of Spiritual Warfare](https://www.bible-knowledge.com/levels-3and4-spiritualwarfare/)

- Sometimes, demons will be allowed to come in and attack, and/or attach to, us as a result of what someone else may have done to us.

- Examples 
	- rape
	- ritual abuse
	- victims of crimes and assault
		- physical abuse
		- verbal abuse

- The demons are looking for a hole in the victim's hedge. These holes are a result of the victim's response is / was to the violation.

- If the victim does not work through the negative emotional reactions, it allows the demons "legal rights" to attack them.
	- As a result, the demons will try to increase the frequency and intensity of these emotions which turns them into mental strongholds.

- In order to become free from these demons, the victim **MUST** fully forgive **ALL** those who have wronged them - no matter how evil and vicious the wrong(s) may have been.
	- Demons feed off of the negative emotions; that's why they try to increase the frequency and intensity of them.
	- If they are unable to forgive, have them ask the Lord to help them to be made willing.  
		- Scripture - work out your salvation with fear and trembling, for it is the Lord who is at work in you both to will and to do according to His good pleasure.
	- If they do not fully forgive those who have hurt them 
		- the demons cannot be cast out.
		- God will not forgive them.
		- God may not be willing to hear any of their prayers which will make deliverance difficult, if not impossible.
