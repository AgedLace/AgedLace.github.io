## Vision of the Four Beasts

### Four Beasts Out of the Sea

#### First Beast

#Daniel-7/Dreams/First-Beast

- like a lion
- wings of an eagle
	- wings torn off
- lifted up from the ground
	- made to stand on two feet like a man
- given the mind of a man

#### Second Beast

#Daniel-7/Dreams/Second-Beast

- like a bear
- raised up on one side
- three ribs between its teeth
- told 'Get up and gorge yourself on flesh'

#### Third Beast

#Daniel-7/Dreams/Third-Beast

- like a leopard
- 4 wings on its back
- 4 heads
- given authority to rule

#### Fourth Beast

#Daniel-7/Dreams/Fourth-Beast

- terrifying -- dreadful
- extremely strong
- large iron teeth
- bronze claws (verse 19)
	- iron teeth and bronze claws are reminiscent of Nebuchadnezzar's statue #Nebuchadnezzar/Dream/Statue-of-Metals  
		- Babylon = gold
		- Medo-Persia = silver
		- Greek = bronze
		- Rome = iron
			- iron mixed with clay
- devoured and crushed
- trampled residue underfoot
- 10 horns #Daniel-7/Dreams/Fourth-Beast/10-horns
	- little horn #Daniel-7/Dreams/Fourth-Beast/Little-Horn  
		- uproots 3 horns
		- eyes of a man
		- mouth
			- speaking arrogant words
		- *Added from verse 19 and following*
		- appearance more imposing than the others
		- waged war against the saints and prevailed
		- **UNTIL**
			- Ancient of Days arrived
				- pronounced judgment
					- in favor of the saints of the Most High
				- **AND**
					- the time came for them (saints) to possess the kingdom

### Interpretation

- Four Beasts are four kings (kingdoms?)
	- will arise from the earth
		- in the dream they came out of the sea, so this indicates the sea represents the (Gentile ?) nations of the earth?
	- Saints of the Most High
		- receive the kingdom
		- and possess it forever
			- yes, forever and ever

- Fourth Beast is a fourth kingdom
	- will appear on the earth
	- different from all the other kingdoms
	- will devour the whole earth
	- trample it down
	- crush it

- 10 horns are 10 kings
	- will rise from this 4th Beast kingdom
	- after these 10 kings
		- another king
			- different from the earlier ones
			- will arise 
			- and subdue three kings
			- will speak out against the Most High
			- will oppress the saints of the Most High
			- *intending* to change the *appointed times and laws*
			- saints given into his hand 
				- for a time, times, and half a time

## Vision of the Ancient of Days

#Daniel-7/Dreams/Ancient-of-Days

### Ancient of Days and Thrones

- thrones set in place
- Ancient of Days takes His seat
	- clothing white as snow
	- hair like pure wool
	- throne flaming with fire
		- throne's wheels ablaze

### Little Horn, Beast Slain and Remaining Beasts

- Little Horn
	- speaking arrogant words

- Beast slain
	- body destroyed
	- thrown into blazing fire

- Remaining Beasts
	- dominion removed
	- granted an extension of life
		- for a season and a time

### Interpretation

- Court will convene
	- his (little horn) dominion taken away
	- and completely destroyed **FOREVER**

#Daniel-7/Dreams/Son-of-Man
