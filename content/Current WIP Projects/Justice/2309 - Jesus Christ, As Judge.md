Jesus Christ executed judgment against the forces of evil through his death on the cross. Individuals will be judged according to their response to his saving grace.

Jesus Christ is appointed judge by God the Father

[John 5:22](https://biblehub.com/john/5-22.htm) _See also_ [John 5:27](https://biblehub.com/john/5-27.htm); [Acts 10:42](https://biblehub.com/acts/10-42.htm); [Acts 17:31](https://biblehub.com/acts/17-31.htm)

Jesus Christ is a just judge

[John 5:30](https://biblehub.com/john/5-30.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 11:3-4](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/11-3.htm); [Micah 4:3](https://biblehub.com/micah/4-3.htm); [John 8:15-16](https://biblehub.com/john/8-15.htm); [Acts 17:31](https://biblehub.com/acts/17-31.htm); [Revelation 19:11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-11.htm)

Jesus Christ's death brought judgment on Satan and evil world powers

[John 12:31-33](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm) _See also_ [John 16:11](https://biblehub.com/john/16-11.htm)

Jesus Christ will act as judge at the end of time

[2 Timothy 4:1](https://biblehub.com/2_timothy/4-1.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 25:31-32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-31.htm); [Romans 2:16](https://biblehub.com/romans/2-16.htm); [2 Thessalonians 1:7-10](https://biblehub.com/2_thessalonians/1-7.htm); [2 Timothy 4:8](https://biblehub.com/2_timothy/4-8.htm); [Revelation 19:11-16](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-11.htm); [Revelation 22:12](https://biblehub.com/revelation/22-12.htm)

People will be judged according to their response to Jesus Christ

[John 3:18](https://biblehub.com/john/3-18.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 10:32-33](https://biblehub.com/matthew/10-32.htm) pp [Luke 12:8-9](https://biblehub.com/luke/12-8.htm); [Mark 8:38](https://biblehub.com/mark/8-38.htm) pp [Luke 9:26](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-26.htm); [John 9:39](https://biblehub.com/john/9-39.htm); [John 12:48](https://biblehub.com/john/12-48.htm)

Jesus Christ's teaching on judgment

Jesus Christ will separate the righteous from the unrighteous

[Matthew 25:31-32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-31.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 3:12](https://biblehub.com/matthew/3-12.htm); [Matthew 13:24-30](https://biblehub.com/matthew/13-24.htm)-42 the parable of the weeds; [Matthew 13:47-50](https://biblehub.com/matthew/13-47.htm) the parable of the net; [Matthew 25:33-46](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-33.htm) the parable of the sheep and the goats

Those who reject Jesus Christ will be punished

[Daniel 2:34-35](https://biblehub.com/daniel/2-34.htm); [Matthew 21:33-44](https://biblehub.com/matthew/21-33.htm) pp [Mark 12:1-11](https://biblehub.com/mark/12-1.htm) pp [Luke 20:9-18](https://biblehub.com/luke/20-9.htm) the parable of the tenants

Christians will stand before Jesus Christ as judge and be rewarded for what they have done

[Matthew 16:27](https://biblehub.com/matthew/16-27.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 25:14-30](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-14.htm) the parable of the talents; [Luke 19:12-27](https://biblehub.com/luke/19-12.htm) the parable of the ten minas; [2 Corinthians 5:10](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/5-10.htm)

The day of Jesus Christ's return as judge is unknown

[Mark 13:32-36](https://biblehub.com/mark/13-32.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 24:36-44](https://biblehub.com/matthew/24-36.htm) pp [Luke 17:26-37](https://biblehub.com/luke/17-26.htm); [Matthew 25:1-13](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-1.htm) the parable of the ten virgins
