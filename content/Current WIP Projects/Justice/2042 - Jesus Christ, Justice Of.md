[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [2000 Jesus Christ](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2000.htm)  "  [2003 Jesus Christ, qualities of](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2003.htm)  "  [2006 God, the Son](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2006.htm)  "  [2042 Jesus Christ, justice of](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2042.htm)

The teaching and lifestyle of Jesus Christ show him to be just and impartial in his judgments and dealings with people. At the end of the age, God has appointed Jesus Christ to judge and rule with justice.

Justice was a quality displayed in Jesus Christ's life

[Matthew 12:18-21](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-18.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 42:1-4](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/42-1.htm)

Jesus Christ was just and impartial in his dealings with people

[John 4:9](https://biblehub.com/john/4-9.htm) with Samaritans; [John 4:27](https://biblehub.com/john/4-27.htm) with women; [Matthew 9:10-12](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-10.htm) pp [Mark 2:15-17](https://biblehub.com/mark/2-15.htm) pp [Luke 5:29-31](https://biblehub.com/luke/5-29.htm) with tax collectors and those classed as "sinners"; [Matthew 15:21-28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/15-21.htm) pp [Mark 7:24-29](https://biblehub.com/mark/7-24.htm) with the Gentiles

Justice in the teaching of Jesus Christ

[Matthew 23:23](https://biblehub.com/matthew/23-23.htm) pp [Luke 11:42](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-42.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 5:17-48](https://biblehub.com/matthew/5-17.htm); [Matthew 7:1-5](https://biblehub.com/matthew/7-1.htm) pp [Luke 6:37-42](https://biblehub.com/luke/6-37.htm); [Luke 12:58-59](https://biblehub.com/luke/12-58.htm); [Luke 14:13-14](https://biblehub.com/luke/14-13.htm); [John 7:24](https://biblehub.com/john/7-24.htm); [John 8:7](https://biblehub.com/john/8-7.htm)

Parables which teach justice

[Luke 18:1-8](https://biblehub.com/luke/18-1.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 20:1-16](https://biblehub.com/matthew/20-1.htm); [Matthew 25:14-30](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-14.htm) pp [Luke 19:12-27](https://biblehub.com/luke/19-12.htm); [Luke 16:19-31](https://biblehub.com/luke/16-19.htm)

Jesus Christ will judge the nations with justice

[Acts 17:31](https://biblehub.com/acts/17-31.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 72:1-2](https://biblehub.com/psalms/72-1.htm) a Messianic psalm; [Isaiah 11:4](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/11-4.htm); [John 5:30](https://biblehub.com/john/5-30.htm); [John 8:15-16](https://biblehub.com/john/8-15.htm); [Revelation 19:11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-11.htm)

Jesus Christ will rule the nations with justice

[Isaiah 9:6-7](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/9-6.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 2:2-4](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/2-2.htm) This prophecy refers to the Messianic age when Jesus Christ will rule the world as king; [Isaiah 32:1](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/32-1.htm)
