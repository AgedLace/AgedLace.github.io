- *deceiver(s) - G4108 - planos* #Greek/G4108-planos
	- **Strong's Concordance**
		- wandering, leading astray, a deceiver
		- misleading, deceiving, wandering; a deceiver, imposter
	- **HELPS Word-studies**
		- **Cognate: 4108** _plános_ (a substantival adjective, derived from [4105](https://biblehub.com/greek/4105.htm) _/planáō_, "wander") – a deceiver, trying to get others to also veer off God's course (path of safety). [See 4105](https://biblehub.com/greek/4105.htm) (_planáo_).
	- **Strong's Exhaustive Concordance**
		- deceiver, seducer
		- Of uncertain affinity; roving (as a tramp), i.e. **(by implication) an impostor or misleader**; --deceiver, seducing.
	- 5 Occurrences in the NT
		- Matthew 27:63
		- 2 Corinthians 6:8
		- 1 Timothy 4:1
		- 2 John 1:7 - 2x

- key phrase - ***refusing** to confess the coming of Jesus Christ in the flesh.*

- *antichrist - G500 - antihchristos* #Greek/G500-antichristos  
	- **Strong's Concordance**
		- antichrist, (one who opposes Christ)
		- either one who puts himself in the place of or, or the enemy (opponent) of the Messiah
	- **HELPS Word-studies**
		- **500** _antíxristos_ (from [473](https://biblehub.com/greek/473.htm) _/antí_, "opposite to, in place of" and [5547](https://biblehub.com/greek/5547.htm) _/Xristós_, "Christ") – properly, opposite to Christ; someone acting _in place of_ (_against_) Christ; "_Antichrist_."
	- 5 Occurrences in the NT; all used by John
		- 1 John 2:18 - 2x
		- 1 John 2:22
		- 1 John 4:3
		- 2 John 1:7
