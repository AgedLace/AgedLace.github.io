[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [5000 Humanity](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5000.htm)  "  [5760 Human attitudes and behaviour](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5760.htm)  "  [5800 blasphemy](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5800.htm)

The profaning, desecration and taking in vain of the name of God, or the reviling of any of his works or deeds. It is strongly forbidden by God as dishonouring to his name.

Blasphemy strongly forbidden

[Exodus 20:7](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-7.htm) pp [Deuteronomy 5:11](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/5-11.htm) _See also_ [Exodus 22:28](https://biblehub.com/exodus/22-28.htm); [Leviticus 18:21](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/18-21.htm); [Leviticus 19:12](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/19-12.htm); [Leviticus 22:32](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/22-32.htm); [Numbers 15:30-31](https://biblehub.com/numbers/15-30.htm)

Blasphemy against God punished

[Leviticus 24:10-16](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/24-10.htm); [2 Kings 19:20-37](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/19-20.htm) pp [Isaiah 37:21-38](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/37-21.htm)

Blasphemy against the Holy Spirit is unpardonable

[Mark 3:28-30](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-28.htm) pp [Matthew 12:31-32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-31.htm) pp [Luke 12:10](https://biblehub.com/luke/12-10.htm) _See also_ [Hebrews 10:29](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/10-29.htm)

God blasphemed indirectly

[Nehemiah 9:26](https://biblehub.com/nehemiah/9-26.htm) _See also_ [2 Chronicles 36:16](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/36-16.htm); [Psalm 107:11](https://biblehub.com/psalms/107-11.htm); [Isaiah 5:24](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/5-24.htm)

[Leviticus 22:1-2](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/22-1.htm) _See also_ [Ezekiel 20:27-28](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/20-27.htm); [Ezekiel 22:26](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/22-26.htm); [Malachi 1:6-13](https://biblehub.com/malachi/1-6.htm)

Despising the poor blasphemes God

[Proverbs 14:31](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/14-31.htm) _See also_ [Amos 2:7](https://biblehub.com/amos/2-7.htm); [James 2:5-7](https://biblehub.com/james/2-5.htm)

[Zephaniah 2:8-11](https://biblehub.com/zephaniah/2-8.htm); [Acts 9:4-5](https://biblehub.com/acts/9-4.htm) To persecute the church is to persecute Jesus Christ; [Acts 26:9](https://biblehub.com/acts/26-9.htm); [1 Timothy 1:13](https://biblehub.com/1_timothy/1-13.htm); [Revelation 2:9](https://biblehub.com/revelation/2-9.htm)

Slandering celestial beings blasphemes God

[2 Peter 2:10-12](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/2-10.htm); Jude 8-10

The sins of God's people may cause others to blaspheme

[1 Timothy 6:1](https://biblehub.com/1_timothy/6-1.htm) _See also_ [2 Samuel 12:13-14](https://biblehub.com/2_samuel/12-13.htm) The phrase "you have made the enemies of the LORD show utter contempt" reflects a scribal tradition which avoided any reference to blasphemy.
