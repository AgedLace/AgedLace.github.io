Demons are subject to the authority of Jesus Christ during his ministry and subsequently in the ministry of the church.

All spiritual powers are subject to Jesus Christ who has supreme authority

[Ephesians 1:19-22](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/1-19.htm) _See also_ [Romans 8:38](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-38.htm); [Ephesians 3:10](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/3-10.htm); [Colossians 1:16](https://biblehub.com/colossians/1-16.htm); [Colossians 2:10](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-10.htm)

**Demons are powerless before Jesus Christ**

Demons can only do what he permits

[Matthew 8:31-32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-31.htm) pp [Mark 5:12-13](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-12.htm) pp [Luke 8:32-33](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-32.htm)

Demons obey his command

[Matthew 17:18](https://biblehub.com/matthew/17-18.htm) pp [Mark 9:25-26](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-25.htm) pp [Luke 9:42](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-42.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 9:33-34](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-33.htm); [Mark 1:34](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-34.htm); [Mark 16:9](https://biblehub.com/mark/16-9.htm); [Luke 11:14](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-14.htm)

Demons disperse at his word

[Mark 9:25](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-25.htm) _See also_ [Mark 1:25](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-25.htm) pp [Luke 4:35](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-35.htm)

**The name of Jesus Christ has power against demons**

Jesus Christ's name is used by his disciples

[Mark 16:17](https://biblehub.com/mark/16-17.htm) _See also_ [Mark 9:38-39](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-38.htm) pp [Luke 9:49-50](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-49.htm); [Luke 10:17](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-17.htm); [Acts 16:18](https://biblehub.com/acts/16-18.htm)

Jesus Christ anticipates the misuse of his own name

[Matthew 7:22-23](https://biblehub.com/matthew/7-22.htm) _See also_ [Acts 19:13-16](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-13.htm) an example where Jesus' name is used in cases of demon-possession--but not by Jesus Christ's disciples, and not successfully

**Jesus Christ's disciples have delegated power over demons**

In Jesus Christ's own lifetime

[Matthew 10:1](https://biblehub.com/matthew/10-1.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 10:7](https://biblehub.com/matthew/10-7.htm); [Mark 3:14-15](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-14.htm); [Mark 6:7](https://biblehub.com/mark/6-7.htm) pp [Luke 9:1](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-1.htm); [Mark 6:13](https://biblehub.com/mark/6-13.htm); [Luke 10:17](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-17.htm)

In the ongoing ministry of the church

[Mark 16:17](https://biblehub.com/mark/16-17.htm) _See also_ [Acts 5:16](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-16.htm); [Acts 8:7](https://biblehub.com/acts/8-7.htm); [Acts 16:18](https://biblehub.com/acts/16-18.htm); [Acts 19:12](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-12.htm)

Others will attempt to drive out demons

[Matthew 12:27](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-27.htm) pp [Luke 11:19](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-19.htm); [Mark 9:38](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-38.htm) pp [Luke 9:49](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-49.htm); [Acts 19:13-15](https://biblehub.com/acts/19-13.htm)

The final punishment of demons

[2 Peter 2:4](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/2-4.htm); Jude 6
