The use of mediums or other spiritual intermediaries to communicate with the dead. Scripture forbids such practices, which are inconsistent with obedience to a living God.

Spiritism forbidden by God

[Leviticus 19:31](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/19-31.htm) _See also_ [Deuteronomy 18:10-12](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/18-10.htm)

Punishment for spiritism

[Leviticus 20:27](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/20-27.htm) _See also_ [Leviticus 20:6](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/20-6.htm)

Spiritism practised in Israel

[2 Kings 21:6](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/21-6.htm) pp [2 Chronicles 33:6](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/33-6.htm) _See also_ [1 Samuel 28:4-20](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/28-4.htm)

Spiritists expelled from Israel

[2 Kings 23:24](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/23-24.htm) _See also_ [1 Samuel 28:3](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/28-3.htm)

The futility of spiritism
