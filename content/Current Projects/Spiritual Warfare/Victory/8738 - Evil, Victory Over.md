Although evil continues to be present and active in the world, Scripture gives an assurance that God will finally triumph over evil in all its forms.

Satan's power is already limited by God

[Job 1:12](https://biblehub.com/job/1-12.htm) _See also_ [Genesis 3:14-15](https://biblehub.com/genesis/3-14.htm); [Job 2:6](https://biblehub.com/job/2-6.htm); [Zechariah 3:2](https://biblehub.com/zechariah/3-2.htm); [Revelation 12:9](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-9.htm)

Evil spirits are subject to God's control

[1 Samuel 16:14](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/16-14.htm) _See also_ [Judges 9:23](https://biblehub.com/judges/9-23.htm); [1 Samuel 18:10](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/18-10.htm); [Samuel 19:9](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/19-9.htm); [1 Kings 22:23](https://biblehub.com/1_kings/22-23.htm); [Colossians 2:13-15](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-13.htm); Jude 6

Jesus Christ's triumph over evil powers

[1 John 3:8](https://biblehub.com/1_john/3-8.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 12:25-29](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-25.htm) pp [Mark 3:23-27](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-23.htm) pp [Luke 11:17-22](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-17.htm); [Mark 1:27](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-27.htm) pp [Luke 4:36](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-36.htm); [Luke 4:13](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-13.htm); [Luke 10:18](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-18.htm); [John 12:31](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm); [John 14:30](https://biblehub.com/john/14-30.htm); [Hebrews 2:14-15](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/2-14.htm); [1 John 4:4](https://biblehub.com/1_john/4-4.htm); [Revelation 3:21](https://biblehub.com/revelation/3-21.htm)

God's transforming power reverses the effects of evil in human lives

[2 Corinthians 3:18](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/3-18.htm); [1 John 3:2](https://biblehub.com/1_john/3-2.htm) _See also_ [Jonah 3:10](https://biblehub.com/jonah/3-10.htm); [Mark 5:15](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-15.htm) pp [Luke 8:35](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-35.htm); [Luke 19:8](https://biblehub.com/luke/19-8.htm); [Acts 26:17-18](https://biblehub.com/acts/26-17.htm); [Romans 6:14](https://biblehub.com/romans/6-14.htm); [Romans 12:2](https://biblehub.com/romans/12-2.htm); [1 Corinthians 15:10](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/15-10.htm); [2 Corinthians 12:9](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/12-9.htm); [Colossians 3:10](https://biblehub.com/colossians/3-10.htm)

God brings good out of evil

[Genesis 50:20](https://biblehub.com/genesis/50-20.htm); [Romans 8:28](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-28.htm) _See also_ [Genesis 45:8](https://biblehub.com/genesis/45-8.htm); [Job 23:10](https://biblehub.com/job/23-10.htm); [John 9:3](https://biblehub.com/john/9-3.htm); [John 12:24](https://biblehub.com/john/12-24.htm); [Acts 2:36](https://biblehub.com/acts/2-36.htm); [Acts 3:13-16](https://biblehub.com/acts/3-13.htm); [Acts 5:30-31](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-30.htm); [Philippians 1:12-14](https://biblehub.com/philippians/1-12.htm)-18; [James 1:2-3](https://biblehub.com/james/1-2.htm); [1 Peter 1:6-7](https://biblehub.com/1_peter/1-6.htm)

The removal of the curse on creation

[Romans 8:20-21](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-20.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 19:28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/19-28.htm); [Acts 3:21](https://biblehub.com/acts/3-21.htm); [Revelation 21:1](https://biblehub.com/revelation/21-1.htm); [Revelation 22:3](https://biblehub.com/revelation/22-3.htm)

Jesus Christ's victory over evil is expressed in his thousand-year reign with believers, during which Satan is bound

[Revelation 20:4](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-4.htm) _See also_ [Revelation 20:2](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-2.htm) Interpreters differ over whether the "thousand years" refers to a future period or to Jesus Christ's present rule in the world.

The final defeat of all evil powers

[Revelation 20:4](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-4.htm); [Romans 16:20](https://biblehub.com/romans/16-20.htm); [Revelation 20:10](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-10.htm) _See also_ [Malachi 4:1](https://biblehub.com/malachi/4-1.htm); [Matthew 13:41-42](https://biblehub.com/matthew/13-41.htm); [Matthew 25:41](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-41.htm); [1 Corinthians 15:25](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/15-25.htm); [2 Thessalonians 2:8](https://biblehub.com/2_thessalonians/2-8.htm); [Revelation 19:20](https://biblehub.com/revelation/19-20.htm); [Revelation 21:8](https://biblehub.com/revelation/21-8.htm)

Evil will be excluded from the new heaven and earth

[2 Peter 3:13](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/3-13.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 65:17](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/65-17.htm); [Revelation 21:4](https://biblehub.com/revelation/21-4.htm); [Revelation 22:15](https://biblehub.com/revelation/22-15.htm)
