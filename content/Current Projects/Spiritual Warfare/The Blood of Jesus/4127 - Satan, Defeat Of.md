Though defeated on Jesus Christ's cross, Satan is still active in this world, but his final overthrow by God is certain.

**Satan is defeated by Jesus Christ**

Jesus Christ is stronger than Satan

[John 14:30](https://biblehub.com/john/14-30.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 12:28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-28.htm); [Luke 13:16](https://biblehub.com/luke/13-16.htm); [Acts 10:38](https://biblehub.com/acts/10-38.htm); [Galatians 1:4](https://biblehub.com/galatians/1-4.htm)

Jesus Christ came to destroy Satan's work

[1 John 3:8](https://biblehub.com/1_john/3-8.htm) _See also_ [Hebrews 2:14-15](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/2-14.htm)

Satan is defeated on the cross

[Colossians 1:13](https://biblehub.com/colossians/1-13.htm); [Titus 2:14](https://biblehub.com/titus/2-14.htm); [Revelation 3:21](https://biblehub.com/revelation/3-21.htm)

Satan's defeat is foreseen by Jesus Christ

[John 12:31](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 25:41](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-41.htm); [Luke 10:18](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-18.htm); [John 16:11](https://biblehub.com/john/16-11.htm)

Satan's agents are also defeated

[Colossians 2:15](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-15.htm) _See also_ [Romans 8:38-39](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-38.htm)

**Satan is defeated by Christians**

[Revelation 12:11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-11.htm) _See also_ [Acts 5:40-42](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-40.htm); [1 John 2:13-14](https://biblehub.com/1_john/2-13.htm)

NOTE - the correct translation of Rev 12:11 is "they overcame **by reason of / because of** the Blood of the Lamb and **by reason of / because of** the word of their testimony."

**Satan's final overthrow**

Satan and his agents will be destroyed

[Romans 16:20](https://biblehub.com/romans/16-20.htm) _See also_ [Revelation 17:14](https://biblehub.com/revelation/17-14.htm)

Satan's final destiny

[1 Timothy 3:6](https://biblehub.com/1_timothy/3-6.htm) judgment

Torment:

[Revelation 20:1-3](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-1.htm)

Death, the result of Satan's work, will be defeated

[1 Corinthians 15:26](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/15-26.htm); [Revelation 20:13-14](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-13.htm)
