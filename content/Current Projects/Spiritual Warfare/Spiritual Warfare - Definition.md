## 8482 spiritual warfare

**SOURCE** - [Dictionary of Bible Themes: 8482 spiritual warfare](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8482.htm)

[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [8000 The life of the believer](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8000.htm)  "  [8400 The tasks of the believer](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8400.htm)  "  [8482 spiritual warfare](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8482.htm)

The struggle against the forces of evil, which is a constant feature of the life of faith. Scripture locates the origins of spiritual warfare in the rebellion of Satan and his angels against God and affirms the hope of God's final victory over such forces through Jesus Christ's death and resurrection.

Spiritual warfare has its origin in a rebellion of many angels against God. Satan is seen as the prince of this world, leading an array of forces opposed to God. Although disarmed by Jesus Christ on the cross, they remain a powerful threat to the church and to individual believers.

## 8484 spiritual warfare, enemies in

**SOURCE** - [Dictionary of Bible Themes: 8484 spiritual warfare, enemies in](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8484.htm)

[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [8000 The life of the believer](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8000.htm)  "  [8400 The tasks of the believer](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8400.htm)  "  [8482 spiritual warfare](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8482.htm)  "  [8484 spiritual warfare, enemies in](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/8484.htm)

Christians are at war with the world, the flesh and the devil. Satan empowers the world to attack believers externally, persecuting, deceiving and seducing them. Internally, sinfulness frustrates the efforts of believers to serve God fully.

The world is an enemy

[James 4:4](https://biblehub.com/james/4-4.htm) "world" here refers to the sinful human race, hostile to the truth and to the people of God. _See also_ [Romans 12:2](https://biblehub.com/romans/12-2.htm); [1 John 2:15-17](https://biblehub.com/1_john/2-15.htm); [John 5:4](https://biblehub.com/1_john/5-4.htm)

The world hates Christians

[John 15:19-20](https://biblehub.com/john/15-19.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 5:10-12](https://biblehub.com/matthew/5-10.htm) pp [Luke 6:22](https://biblehub.com/luke/6-22.htm); [John 16:2](https://biblehub.com/john/16-2.htm); [John 17:14](https://biblehub.com/john/17-14.htm); [1 Peter 4:12-16](https://biblehub.com/1_peter/4-12.htm); [1 John 3:13](https://biblehub.com/1_john/3-13.htm); [Revelation 13:7](https://biblehub.com/revelation/13-7.htm) The beast may represent the world's antichristian persecution.

The world has its own false teaching and religion

The world seeks to seduce Christians

[1 John 2:16](https://biblehub.com/1_john/2-16.htm) _See also_ [Luke 12:15](https://biblehub.com/luke/12-15.htm); [2 Timothy 4:10](https://biblehub.com/2_timothy/4-10.htm); [Titus 2:12](https://biblehub.com/titus/2-12.htm); [Hebrews 11:24-25](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/11-24.htm); [Revelation 17:1-5](https://biblehub.com/revelation/17-1.htm) Babylon may represent the world's seduction.

## 8485 spiritual warfare, as conflict
