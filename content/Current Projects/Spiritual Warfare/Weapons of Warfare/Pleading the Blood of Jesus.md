#### Protection 

##### Two Ways 

- Preventively 
	- Pleading the Blood for protection **BEFORE** attacks or adversity comes against you.
- For Deliverance
	- When you need God to deliver and/or heal you in order to set you free.

## Why Plead the Blood of Jesus

- It is a powerful form of **offensive** prayer

- OT Example
	- During the Passover, God told the Israelites to apply the blood of the sacrificial lamb on their door posts and lintels. This shed blood protected everyone inside that house from the angel of death sent to kill the firstborn of all the Egyptians.

## Requirements Before Pleading the Blood of Jesus

- In order to qualify to apply the Blood of Jesus you **MUST** be in a covenant relationship with God by believing and accepting Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior.
	- you **MUST** be saved and born-again.
	- a non-believer **cannot** use the Blood of Jesus
	- you **MUST** be fully surrendered, and yielded to God; this includes …
		- your body
		- your soul
		- your spirit
		- your **entire** life
	- any sin **MUST** be confessed to God before God can grant you forgiveness.

## How To Plead the Blood of Jesus 

 

### Preventively

- What do you want God's protection for? Below are a list of the basics / minimums you should pray over.
	- your person
		- body, soul, and spirit
	- your household
		- spouse, children, etc.
	- your possessions and property
		- house, car, finances (including investments, banks, credit / debit cards, identity theft prevention, ... ) etc.
	- your work, school, etc
		- office, class, etc
	- whatever else you want God's protection for; **BE SPECIFIC**
		- Trains and Planes
			- right before you board, or the night before

- In order to get **full** protection, you need to ...
	- Plead the Blood **ON** the object you want protected
	- Plead the Blood **AGAINST** whatever possibly could come against that object
	- Then **SEAL THE PRAYER WITH A STATEMENT OF FAITH AND BELIEF**
	- Pray this prayer over **EACH** category individually.  

> [!NOTE] **Example Prayer to Plead Blood of Jesus**
> 
> Father,
> 
> in the name of Jesus, I now plead the Blood of Jesus on …………. 
> 
> I now Plead the Blood of Jesus against …………….
> 
> Father, in the name of Jesus, I have full faith and belief that the Blood of Jesus will protect me against any of the things that I have just Pled His Blood on.
> 
> Thank you Father.\
> Thank you Jesus.

### Deliverance

- First, ask the Holy Spirit to Help You Create a Battle Strategy
- Then go on the **offensive** ...
	- Plead the Blood of Jesus directly against whatever the adversity / attack is coming against you
	- The Blood **MUST** be specifically pled against whatever nature the attack is.

###### God will Dry up the Waters of the Enemy

- Waters are important to God
	- John 7:38 - "out of our heart will flow rivers of living water."
	- Ephesians 5:26 - "with the washing of water by the word."

- Demons look for water in which to feed and drink from the spiritual realm.
	- Matthew 12:43 - when demons leave a person, they go through "dry places," and "seek rest," and they find "none."
	- Because they do not find rest in the dry places, they do not like dry places.

- What is the 'water' they are drinking from on the inside of a person?
	- the **legal rights** that allowed theme entrance
- Once you break all of their legal rights, you will dry up all of their waters.
- Once you dry up all of their waters, you will severely weaken them and they will have nothing else left to be able to feed and drink on
- Weakening the demons in this way, and they will be much easier to cast out once you start to directly engage with them.

- Example -
