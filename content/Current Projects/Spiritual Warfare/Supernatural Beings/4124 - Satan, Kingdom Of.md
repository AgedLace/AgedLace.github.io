Satan is depicted as the prince of this world, having power over demons and over godless people. He is, however, subject to God's sovereignty and therefore his power is limited and his rule temporary.

Satan's power

He has power in the world

[1 John 5:19](https://biblehub.com/1_john/5-19.htm) _See also_ [John 7:7](https://biblehub.com/john/7-7.htm); [John 8:44](https://biblehub.com/john/8-44.htm); [John 14:17](https://biblehub.com/john/14-17.htm); [John 15:18-19](https://biblehub.com/john/15-18.htm); [John 16:20](https://biblehub.com/john/16-20.htm); [John 17:14](https://biblehub.com/john/17-14.htm); [Acts 26:18](https://biblehub.com/acts/26-18.htm); [2 Corinthians 4:4](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/4-4.htm); [Ephesians 2:2](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/2-2.htm); [Colossians 1:13](https://biblehub.com/colossians/1-13.htm); [1 John 2:15-16](https://biblehub.com/1_john/2-15.htm); [John 4:4-5](https://biblehub.com/1_john/4-4.htm); [Revelation 13:12](https://biblehub.com/revelation/13-12.htm)

He claims power over this world

[Matthew 4:8-9](https://biblehub.com/matthew/4-8.htm) pp [Luke 4:6](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-6.htm)

He is called the "prince" or the "god" of this world

[John 12:31](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm)

These titles indicate the temporary nature of Satan's rule:

[John 14:30](https://biblehub.com/john/14-30.htm); [John 16:11](https://biblehub.com/john/16-11.htm); [2 Corinthians 4:4](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/4-4.htm)

He is responsible for some illnesses

[Luke 13:16](https://biblehub.com/luke/13-16.htm) _See also_ [Job 2:7](https://biblehub.com/job/2-7.htm); [Acts 10:38](https://biblehub.com/acts/10-38.htm); [2 Corinthians 12:7](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/12-7.htm) Paul's "thorn in the flesh" may or may not have been a physical illness.

He is responsible for death

[Hebrews 2:14](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/2-14.htm)

Satan's kingdom

He has a kingdom

[Matthew 12:26](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-26.htm) pp [Luke 11:18](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-18.htm) [Colossians 1:13](https://biblehub.com/colossians/1-13.htm) _See also_ [Acts 26:18](https://biblehub.com/acts/26-18.htm); [Ephesians 2:2](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/2-2.htm)

He has a throne

[Revelation 2:13](https://biblehub.com/revelation/2-13.htm)

He is the prince of demons

[Matthew 9:34](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-34.htm) The name Beelzebub is used elsewhere to name the prince of the demons. This name was used also to identify Satan. _See also_ [Matthew 12:24](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-24.htm) pp [Mark 3:22](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-22.htm) pp [Luke 11:15](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-15.htm)

Satan's authority is limited

He cannot do more than God allows

[Job 1:12](https://biblehub.com/job/1-12.htm) _See also_ [Job 2:6](https://biblehub.com/job/2-6.htm); [1 Corinthians 10:13](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/10-13.htm); [Revelation 12:12](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-12.htm); [Revelation 20:3](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-3.htm)

His rule is temporary

[Romans 16:20](https://biblehub.com/romans/16-20.htm); [Revelation 20:10](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-10.htm)
