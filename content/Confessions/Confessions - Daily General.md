## Daily General Confessions

**Source**: [We're Done! How to Get Over Fear - Terradez Ministries](https://terradezministries.com/2021/02/03/how-to-get-over-fear/?mc_cid=cf02437d04)

---

> I am who God says I am.  
> I have what He says I have.  
> I can do what He says I can do.  
> I have all the money I need,  
> all the resources I need,  
> all the favor I need,  
> all the opportunity I need,  
> all the time I need  
> to do everything God has called me to do.
> 
> I do not lack for inspiration.  
> I do not lack for vision.  
> I do not lack for wisdom.  
> I do not lack for understanding.
> 
> I have the peace of God,  
> the presence of God,  
> the power of God,  
> the purpose of God,  
> and the protection of God working in my life.
> 
> I believe, therefore I receive every good thing which He has set aside for me today!
