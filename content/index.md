## Purpose

There are a few reasons for this Bible Study Notebook

* First, Scripture requires we accurately handle God's Word

	* [[2-Timothy-2v15]]

		* "*Make every effort*" in the Greek means

			* **Strong's Greek 4704**: **To hasten, be eager, be zealous**. From spoude; to use speed, i.e. To make effort, be prompt or earnest.

		* "*who accurately handles*" in the Greek means …

			* **Strong's Greek 3718**: From a compound of orthos and the base of tomoteros, to make a straight cut, i.e. **to dissect correctly**.

* Second, Jesus specifically warned we should not allow ourselves to be deceived by anyone. The only way to be obedient to this command is to diligently read, and study for ourselves, what God's Word **really** says.  

	* [[Matthew-24v4]]

	* [[Mark-13v5]]  

* Third, Jesus commanded us to …

	* Make disciples and teach them to obey everything He has commanded

		* [[Matthew-28v18-20]]

	* Equip others for their works of ministry, to build them up in Christ, and to teach them not be deceived.

		* [[Ephesians-4v11-14]]
