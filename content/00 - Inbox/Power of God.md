## Power of God

You CAN minister to others before you are healed.

The way to guarantee healing if you're sick is to start praying for other people. Why? Because it's the law of sowing and reaping.

Matthew 15:1 ->

* usually the traditions of men will transgress the commandment of God.
* verse 6 - they made the commandment of God of none effect by their tradition
* failure occurs for only 2 reasons …
	* unbelief
		* if you're a believer, unbelief should not be your problem
		* there is no such thing as an unbelieving believer
	* you have a tradition or belief that nullifies the Word of God
* ***If you are not having the results that Jesus had, or the results He has promised, it is a tradition you have accepted somewhere.***

* The Word of God is the ultimate and final authority

			* How to increase in this spiritual hunger?
				* increase energy output that will require greater input
				* it means doing things you've never done

				* read the word more regularly
				* push yourself to do it, develop it, then eventually, you'll desire it
				* same with praying; force yourself to pray

		* listening prayer
		* practicing the presence of God
			* focus and practice on the fact that Jesus lives in you everywhere you go
	* fellowship with like-minded Christians

		* get around people that believe like you do

			* I have an anointing that abides within me

			* Right now, I'm going to release the power of God into "this" specific situation - FOCUS!
			* ***KEY*** - once you release do NOT keep going back to it
				* When it comes back you tell it … "IT'S DONE!"

## Power of Praise

> [!example] ***Psalm 8:2***  
> <sup>***2***</sup> From the mouths of children and infants  
> You have ordained praise<sup>***b***</sup>  
> on account of Your adversaries,  
> to silence the enemy and avenger.
> 
> ***Footnotes***  
> <sup>***b***</sup> Literally *You have ordained strength.* LXX *You have prepared praise;* cited in Matthew 21:16

***KEY*** God has ordained praise to silence the enemy and avenger.

***KEY*** The Hebrew for "praise" is

* H5797 - oz which means *strength* or *might*
	* boldness, loud, might, power, strength, strong
	* Or (fully) rowz (oze); from *azaz*; strength in various applications (force, security, majesty, praise) -- boldness, loud, might, power, strength, strong.

[[Bible-Study/Word Studies/Hebrew/H3245 - yasad]]

* The word "praise" literally means "strength" … and the purpose of it is to silence the enemy and avenger!!!
* So … praise shuts the mouths of God's (and) our adversaries!!! This also aligns with Isaiah 54:17 … 17 No weapon formed against you shall prosper, and you will refute every tongue that accuses you. This is the heritage of the servants of the LORD, and their vindication is from Me,”

	

* THIS is our heritage … we need to learn to praise loud and strong far more often than we do … THIS is why God always commanded the Levites to march in front of the armies and praise …

Compare this with 2 Chronicles 20:21 Then Jehoshaphat consulted with the people and appointed those who would sing to the LORD and praise the splendor of His holiness. As they went out before the army, they were singing: “Give thanks to the LORD, for His loving devotion endures forever.” 22 The moment they began their shouts and praises, the LORD set ambushes against the men of Ammon, Moab, and Mount Seir who had come against Judah, and they were defeated.

"perfected praise" = stops the enemy AND the avenger
