## Authority of Believers Course

* As He is, so are we in this world.
* All authority in heaven and earth has been given to Me (Jesus).
	* Because I am HID IN CHRIST, all the authority He has, I have.  
	* It is no longer I that lives, but CHRIST LIVES IN ME …
	* I am seated with Him in the heavenly places.
	* The works that He did, I shall do - and greater works also.
	* If the devil has ANY authority, then Jesus doesn't have ALL authority
* Don't confuse ability with authority. Just because someone has the ability to do something doesn't mean they have the authority to do so.
* I am SENT by Him to GO IN HIS NAME
	* "Go ye therefore … "
	* … therefore I am His ambassador.
	* As His ambassador I carry, and operate in, His authority.
* Acts 3:1-ff
	* Peter said "Look on us …"
	* "… such as I have, give I thee …"
	* "IN THE NAME OF JESUS CHRIST … RISE UP AND WALK"
	* took him by the hand
		* and lifted him up …
			* IMMEDIATELY (after being lifted up)
			* the man wasn't healed until AFTER Peter had lifted him up
	* Peter takes the opportunity to start preaching …
		* why do you look so earnestly upon us as though by our own power our holiness this man was healed by us, or by our power
		* it's NOT us, or OUR power, that healed this man
		* HIS NAME, THROUGH FAITH IN HIS NAME, has made this man strong
* "Faith for healing" is NEVER mentioned in the Epistles.
* Enforce the Name of Jesus
