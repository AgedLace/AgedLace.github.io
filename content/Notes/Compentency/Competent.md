## Competent

#Competent-Competency

### Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary

1. (a.) Qualified; adequate; sufficient; suitable; capable; legally fit.
2. (a.) Rightfully or properly belonging; incident; -- followed by to.

### Greek

- *G2425 - hikanos* #Greek/G2425-hikanos
	- 2425. hikanos -- sufficient, fit
		- … From hiko (hikano or hikneomai, akin to heko) (to arrive); competent (as if coming
		- in season), ie Ample (in amount) or fit (in character) -- able, + content …
	- **HELPS Word-studies**
		- 2425 *hikanós* (an adjective, derived from the root, *hikō*, "arrive, come to") – "properly, reach to (attain); hence, ***adequate, sufficient***" (J. Thayer).
- *G2427 - hikanoó* #Greek/G2427-hikanoó
	- 2427. hikanoó -- to make sufficient
		- … fit, qualify. Cognate: 2427 -- properly, reaching the place of and hence
		- making someone , ie (competent). See 2425 (). Word Origin …
	- **HELPS Word-studies**
		- Cognate: 2427 *hikanóō* – properly, reaching the place of *sufficiency* and hence making someone *qualified*, i.e. *able* (competent). See 2425 (*hikanos*).
