## Overcome

#Overcame-Overcome-Overcomer 

### Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary

1. (p. p.) of Overcome.
2. (v. t.) To get the better of; to surmount; to conquer; to subdue; as, to overcome enemies in battle.
3. (v. t.) To overflow; to surcharge.
4. (v. t.) To come or pass over; to spreads over.
5. (v. i.) To gain the superiority; to be victorious.
