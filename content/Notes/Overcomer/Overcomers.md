## Overcomers

#Overcame-Overcome-Overcomer

### Greek

- *G3528 - nikaó* #Greek/G3528-nikaó
	- to conquer, prevail
		- from *nike*
	- **HELPS Word-studies**
		- 3528 *nikáō*(from 3529 /*níkē*, "victory") – properly, *conquer* (overcome); " 'to carry off the victory, come off victorious.' **The verb implies a battle**" (K. Wuest).

#NT/1-John-5v4 

> [!note] **1 John 5:4**
> 
> 4 because **everyone** born of God **overcomes the world**. And this is **the victory** that **has overcome** the world: our faith.

#NT/1-John-4v4 

> [!note] **1 John 4:4**
> 
> 4 You, little children, are from God and **have overcome** them, because greater is He who is in you than he who is in the world.
