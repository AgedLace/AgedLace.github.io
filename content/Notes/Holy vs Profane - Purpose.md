## Holy Vs Profane - Purpose

### Purpose

- The reason the priests must distinguish between the holy and the common (profane), between the clean and the unclean, was so that they might teach the Israelites all the statues that the LORD had given them through Moses.

> 
> 10 You **must** distinguish between the holy and the common, between the clean and the unclean,
> 
> 11 **so that you may teach the Israelites all the statutes that the LORD has given them** through Moses."
