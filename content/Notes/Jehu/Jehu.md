## Jehu

- *Jehu - H3058 - Yehu* #Hebrew/H3058-yehu
	- Jehovah is He

### Elijah Anoints Jehu King of Israel

> **1 Kings 19:15-16**
> 
> 15 Then the LORD said to him (Elijah), "Go back by the way you came, and go to the Desert of Damascus. When you arrive, you are to anoint Hazael as king over Aram."
> 
> 16 **You are also to anoint Jehu son of Minshi as king over Israel** and Elisha son of Shaphat from Abel-meholah to succeed you as prophet.

*Q* - Is there anywhere else in Scripture when God physically anointed a new king over a foreign nation?

- Notice that God told Elijah to anoint three people
	- The new king of Aram
	- Jehu as king of Israel
	- Elisha as his successor

### Jehu's Orders From God

> **1 Kings 19:17-18**
> 
> 17 Then Jehu will put to death whoever escapes the sword of Hazael, and Elisha will put to death whoever escapes the sword ofJehu.
> 
> 18 Nevertheless, I have reserved seven thousand in Israel -- all whose knees have not bowed to Baal and whose mouths have not kissed him.

- WOAH!!
	- First the sword of Hazael
	- Then the sword of Jehu
	- Then Elijah puts to death those who escape the first two swords

### Jehu Did Not Turn Away From The Sins Of Jeroboam

> **2 Kings 10:29**
> 
> 29 However Jehu did not turn away from the sins of Jeroboam the son of Nebat, who had made Israel sin, that is, from the golden calves that were at Bethel and Dan.

### Jehu Took No Heed To Walk In the Law of The LORD God of Israel With All His Heart

> **2 Kings 10:31**
> 
> 31 But Jehu took no heed to walk in the law of the LORD God of Israel with all his heart; for he did not depart from the sins of Jeroboam, who had made Israel sin.

### Jehu Reigned 28 Years

> **2 Kings 10:36**
> 
> 36 And the period that Jehu reigned over Israel in Samaria was twenty-eight years.
