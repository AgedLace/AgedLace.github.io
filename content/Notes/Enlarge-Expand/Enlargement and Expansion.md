**Prayer of Jabez**  
#OT/1-Chronicles-4v10

> [!note] **1 Chronicles 4:10**
> 
> 10 And Jabez called out to the God of Israel, "If only You would bless me and enlarge my territory! May Your hand be with me and keep me from harm, so that I will be free from pain."

So, I need to ask You to expand my borders / territory

Lord, over and above any / all enlargement and expansion, I need an increased capacity for, and knowledge of, YOU … I need more of YOU … I require more of YOU. I need to have You living in every part of my being … that there would be nothing of me, but only YOU living through me. I need an increased enlargement and expansion of my capacity to both hear and obey Your voice over all others … I need an increased ability to flow and operate in all of the gifts of Your Holy Spirit living in, and through me … I require this so that I can minister to others in ways that will only bring You glory, honor, praise, and thanksgiving.

Everything else is merely the outflow of You abiding and living in me so thoroughly that I would be truly be Your ambassador of Christ to everyone around me.  

O, Lord God, How I need You … I require You … because without You I am utterly and totally powerless, and miserably evil because there is no good thing in me. Please empower me to BE all You created me to be; please empower me to DO all You created me to do. Not my will, but THINE be done, Lord … on earth as it is in heaven.  

I require a proper fear and respect for You and Who You are - I require Your Blood Covering to cleanse me of all my filthiness and sin. I require You to create in me a CLEAN heart, O God, and for You to renew a RIGHT spirit within me … a spirit of Righteousness … the Righteousness of my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ of Nazareth; that I would truly be clothed with Christ Jesus Himself - that no one would be able to see, or even recognize, 'me' because all they can see or find in me is YOU!

O Lord God, I require the ability to obey Your every word; that obeying You would motivate every single thought, word and deed of my existence from this day forward.

I require the faith that is exercised through Christ Jesus; faith to believe **every single Word** You have spoken and given to us. I require that **living faith** which produces nothing except glory, honor, praise, and thanksgiving to YOU alone; and which produces such a super-abundant harvest that nothing the world has ever seen compares … Oh Lord God … I desire a harvest of souls for You … I desire to bring as many people to You as possible … 

so I required that You enlarge and expand my capacity to receive Your revelation, wisdom, knowledge, insight, illumination, inspiration, discernment … that I can operate effectively and efficiently in You, through You, by You.

I require an enlarged and expanded capacity first to LOVE You in Word and deed, and second LOVE for the masses of humanity; Your LOVE which You have shed abroad in my heart … I ask that You would enlarge and expand it to be so great that it matches the LOVE of Christ … because it **IS** His Love being shed abroad through me.  

Father, I also require the mindset that I would think, say, or do anything that would dishonor You in any way. That even every breath I take would be so focused upon bringing You honor that there is no room for anything else.

Father, I also require an enlarged and expanded ability to live and walk in the power and authority that Jesus has provided and given me. I require You to show me what power and authority is rightfully mine to exercise; and I require You to reveal to me what is not authorized. I require You to do in me **EVERYTHING** because I cannot do anything apart from You. Cause me to do nothing outside of this paradigm - that I am the branch and You are the Vine … that my EVERYTHING comes from You - I am merely Your instrument … Your vessel … 

And Father, 

I am asking You to restore my youth, and the years that the locusts have eaten. Recreate and regenerate my body, my health, my stamina, my strength, my abilities - including my mind - my ability to think clearly and effectively and efficiently; … cause me to be wise, discerning, full of YOUR wisdom, knowledge, revelation, understanding, illumination, inspiration; cause me to be stronger, more agile and flexible physically and mentally; cause me to be wholly dedicated and devoted to You … O My Lord … I know I don't deserve this because I have abused and defiled Your temple - my body. I am sorry … I require You to change my mindset towards my body, towards my work, towards my duty to You … I require You to work in me the ability to will and to act on behalf of Your good purpose … I require You to do it because I cannot, or will not, unless You do it all.  

Oh, holy God … I fall down before You and I offer up everything I am, everything I have, to You … for You to do with as You see fit… 

Lord - once again I pray …  
Lord, Here Am I … Lord, Send Me, Lord, Use Me As You See Fit … cause me to be a vessel which You can use as You see fit in order to accomplish all that You desire to do in and through me.  

O LORD GOD, change my mindset that everything I think, say and do is nothing but YOU and what YOU want to do. Yes, Lord, I need You to set my face like flint upon You and what You want me to do so that absolutely nothing will be able to stop me from doing what You are calling me to do.  

I submit to Your refining fire and purging process, Father. I come before You and willingly submit to this so that my life can be effective and efficient and productive for You.

Open the eyes of my heart, Lord … I want to see You!!!
