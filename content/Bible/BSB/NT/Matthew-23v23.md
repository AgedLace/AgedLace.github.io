> 
> <sup>***23***</sup> Woe to you, scribes and Pharisees, you hypocrites! You pay tithes of mint, dill and cumin. But you have disregarded the weightier matters of the law: justice, mercy, and faithfulness. You should have practiced the latter, without neglecting the former.
