## Spiritual Warfare

* … Get behind me, Satan! You are in my way; you are an offense, a hindrance and a snare to me; for you are minding what partakes not of the nature and quality of God, but of men. - Matthew 16:23 AMPC
* I have the power to tread upon ALL serpents and scorpions. I have the power to tread over ALL the ability of the enemy. NOTHING shall by any means hurt me.
