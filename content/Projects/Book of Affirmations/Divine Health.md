## Divine Health
 

* In every way, I am prosperous and I enjoy good health even as my soul also prospers. - III John 2
* And He said to me, Daughter, your faith (that is your trust and confidence in Me springing from faith in God) HAS restored you to health. GO in (to) peace, and BE CONTINUALLY HEALED and FREE from your (distressing bodily) disease. - Mark 5:34 AMPC
	* Body - BE CONTINUALLY HEALED! Head-to-Toe
	* Body - BE CONTINUALLY FREE FROM ALL DISEASE!
	* Body - You are 137 lbs; no more and no less
	* Body - By His stripes you WERE HEALED
	* Pain - GO! NOW!
	* Toxins - GO! NOW! WITHOUT ANY HARMFUL SIDE-EFFECTS
	* Inflammation - GO! NOW! With NO Harmful Side-effects
