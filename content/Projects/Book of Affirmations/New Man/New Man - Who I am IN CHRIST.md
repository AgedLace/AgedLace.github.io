## New Man - Who I Am ***In Christ***

* For freedom Christ HAS set me free. Therefore, I choose to keep on standing firm and keep on refusing to be subject again to a yoke of bondage. - Galatians 5:1 Wuest.
* My partnership in the faith becomes effective as I fully acknowledge every good thing that is mine in Christ. - Philemon 1:6
