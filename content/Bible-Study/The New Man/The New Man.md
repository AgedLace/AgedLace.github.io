Anointing  
Sonship / Adoption

* John 2:20 - You have an 'unction'
* 1 John 2:22 - The anointing abides in you
	* THEREFORE you're ALWAYS anointed
	* it can only decrease / increase as more or less of 'you' dies
* Luke 4:16 - ff
	* The Spirit of the Lord is upon Me because I am anointed to …
* Galatians 3:
	* who has bewitched you … that you should not obey the truth …
	* received ye the Spirit by the works of the law, or by the hearing of faith
	* "BEGUN in the Spirit"
	* only those who are of faith are of the children of Abraham … because God does not count ANYTHING done in the flesh
	* as many as are under the law, are under THE CURSE
	* the just shall live by faith
	* CHRIST HAS REDEEMED US FROM THE CURSE OF THE LAW
	* the promise of God was made to ONE seed - even CHRIST
	* The New Covenant is with Christ, by way of Abraham … NOT with us directly …
	* the law only lasted until the SEED - CHRIST came …
	* when we are IN CHRIST - we are IN THE SEED OF PROMISE
	* the HEIR, when grown up, IS LORD OF ALL
	* JESUS IS LORD OF LORDS … we are the LORDS He is over … we have been made KINGS and priests
	* when you're no longer a child, you are no longer under bondage to the elements of this world
	* BECAUSE you are a son, He sent His Spirit to live in you
* SPIRIT COMES BECAUSE OF THE ANOINTING - not the other way around
	* BEING A SON (BORN-AGAIN) MEANS BEING ANOINTED
* 1 Samuel 10:1-ff
	* Saul was anointed first THEN the Spirit came upon him
		* he became a 'new man' and he prophesied
	* Saul was anointed with oil BECAUSE God had already anointed him to be Captain over His inheritance
* John 1:12 - AS MANY AS RECEIVED HIM GAVE HE AUTHORITY TO BECOME THE SONS OF GOD
* ANOINTING is used of separating something FROM something TO something else
* ANOINTED = TO BE APPOINTED TO A POSITION
* WHEN YOU ARE APPOINTED TO THE POSITION OF A SON
* AUTHORITY COMES FIRST
* ABILITY (POWER) COMES AFTER
* DO AS OCCASION SERVES YOU …
* Galatians 4:6
	* BECAUSE YOU ARE SONS - GOD HAS SENT THE SPIRIT INTO YOUR HEARTS … YOU ARE NO LONGER A SERVANT BUT A SON, AND IF A SON, AN HEIR OF GOD THROUGH CHRIST
	* Maturity = doing what needs to be done without waiting to be told to do it
