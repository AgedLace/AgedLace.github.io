## G4205 - Pornos

**Source**: [Strong's Greek: 4205. πόρνος (pornos) -- a fornicator](https://biblehub.com/greek/4205.htm)

---

* *s**exually immoral***

	* [[G4205 - pornos]]

		* a fornicator

		* **4205** *pórnos* (from *pernaō*, "to sell off") – properly, a male prostitute. [4205](https://biblehub.com/greek/4205.htm) (*pórnos*) is "*properly*, 'a male prostitute' (so Xen., etc.); *in the NT*, *any fornicator*" (*Abbott-Smith*); i.e. anyone engaging in sexual immorality. [See 4202](https://biblehub.com/greek/4202.htm) (*porneia*).
