## G0727 - Harpax

**Source**: [Strong's Greek: 727. ἅρπαξ (harpax) -- rapacious](https://biblehub.com/greek/727.htm)

---

* ***swindler***
	* [[G0727 - harpax]]
	* rapacious
		* American Heritage Dictionary
			* Having or showing a strong or excessive desire to acquire money or possess things; greedy.
			* **Living by killing prey**, especially in large numbers.
			* **Taking things by force; plundering**.
	* Cognate: 727 hárpaks – **properly, seizing**; a sudden snatching (like in a robbery). See 726 (harpazō).
