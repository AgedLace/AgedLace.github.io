* Repentance
	* John called it ***Baptism with water unto repentance***
	* "A ***baptism of repentance for the remission of sins"
	* John the Baptist and Jesus both preached repentance
		* [[Matt-3]]
		* [[Matt-4]]
		* [[Mark-1]]
		* [[Luke-3]]
	* Jesus said He came to call sinners to repentance
		* [[Matt-9v13]]
		* [[Mark-2]]
		* [[Luke-5]]
	* Jesus rebuked cities because they did not repent
		* [[Matt-11v20-21]]
		* [[Matt-12v41]]
		* [[Luke-10]]
		* [[Luke-11]]
	* Jesus commanded the Disciples to preach repentance in His Name.
		* [[Mark-6v12]]
		* [[Luke-24]]
	* Repentance bears fruit
		* [[Matt-3]]
		* [[Luke-3]]
	* Without Repentance you will perish
		* [[Luke-13]]
	* Joy in heaven, and in the presence of angels over one sinner's repentance
		* [[Luke-15]]
	* Water Baptism comes after the act of repentance
		* [[Acts-2]]
	* Repentance comes at / near conversion - for the blotting out of sin and times of refreshing come from the presence of the Lord
		* [[Acts-2]]
	* God gave Jesus … to give repentance to Israel and forgive their sins
		* [[Acts-5]]
	* God has also granted 'repentance to life' to the Gentiles
		* [[Acts-11]]
	* John's preaching described as 'the baptism of repentance to all the people of Israel'
		* [[Acts-13]]
		* [[Act-19]]
	* God now commands all men everywhere to repent
		* [[Acts-17]]
	* Paul taught "repentance toward God and faith toward our Lord Jesus Christ"
		* [[Acts-20]]
		* [[Acts-26]]
	* Once we have repented and turned to God, we should "do w orks befitting repentance"
		* [[Acts-26]]
	* The goodness of God leads us to repentance
		* [[Romans-2]]
		* He uses the Holy Spirit to convict us of our sin when we hear (and believe) the Word of Truth
	* Our sorrow (for our sin) leads us to repentance
		* [[2-Corinthians-7]]
	* Godly sorrow produces repentance - leading to salvation
		* [[2-Corinthians-7]]
	* godly sorrow is not the same as regret, or the sorrow of the world, which produces death
		* [[2-Corinthians-7]]
	* repentance comes from God
		* [[2-Timothy-2]]
	* "repentance from dead works"
		* [[Hebrews-6]]
	* there a a certain group who fall away who cannot be renewed again to repentance
		* [[Hebrews-6]]
	* There are those who diligently seek for repentance, like Esau, but do not find it
		* [[Hebrews-12]]
	* Jesus' warnings to the 7 churches in Revelation
		* remember therefore from where you have fallen; repent and do the first works, or else I will come to you quickly and remove your lampstand from its place --unless you repent.
		* Repent, or else I will come to you quickly and will fight against them with the sword of My mouth
		* And I gave her time to repent of her sexual immorality, and she did not repent.
		* Indeed I will case her into a sickbed, and those who commit adultery with her into great tribulation, unless they repent of their deeds.
		* Remember therefore how you have received and heard; hold fast and repent. Therefore if you will not watch, I will come upon you as a thief, and you will not know what hour I will come upon you.
		* As many as I love, I rebuke and chasten. Therefore be zealous and repent.
