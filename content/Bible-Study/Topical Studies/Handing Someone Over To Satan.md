### **Summary**

Paul teaches that in order to keep the Body pure, the Church must expel anyone in the Body that is …

* sexually immoral

* greedy

* an idolater

* a verbal abuser

* drunkard

* swindler

### Summary

Paul handed Hymenaeus and Alexander over to Satan that they might be taught not to blaspheme.

In 2 Timothy 4:14-15 Paul tells how Alexander caused him much harm. He warns Timothy to beware of him, too, because "Alexander **vigorously opposed our message**. "
