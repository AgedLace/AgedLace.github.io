## Ask

### **Passage**

![[Bible/BSB/NT/Matthew-6v5-8]]

* When you pray …

	* do not be like the hypocrites

	* go into your inner room, shut your door, and pray to your Father

	* do not babble on like pagans

* Father

	* pray to Him

	* is unseen

	* rewards

	* knows what you need before you ask

![[Bible/BSB/NT/Matthew-7v7-12]]

* Ask

	* it will be given you

* Seek

	* you will find

* Knock

	* the door will be opened to you

* Everyone

	* who asks receives

	* who seeks finds

	* who knocks, door will be opened

* Father

	* in heaven

	* gives good things to those who ask Him

* In everything

	* Do to others as you would have them do to you

		* THIS is the essence of the Law and the Prophets
