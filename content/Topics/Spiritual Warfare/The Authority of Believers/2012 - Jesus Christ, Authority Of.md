[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [2000 Jesus Christ](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2000.htm)  "  [2003 Jesus Christ, qualities of](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2003.htm)  "  [2006 God, the Son](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2006.htm)  "  [2012 Jesus Christ, authority of](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/2012.htm)

The right of Jesus Christ to speak and act on his Father's behalf in forgiving sin, pronouncing judgment and promising eternal life to those who believe in him.

Jesus Christ's authority underlines his divine status

[John 8:28](https://biblehub.com/john/8-28.htm) _See also_ [John 8:58](https://biblehub.com/john/8-58.htm); [John 14:6](https://biblehub.com/john/14-6.htm); [1 Corinthians 1:24](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/1-24.htm); [Colossians 2:10](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-10.htm)

**The origin of Jesus Christ's authority**

It is given by God the Father

[John 3:35](https://biblehub.com/john/3-35.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 28:18](https://biblehub.com/matthew/28-18.htm); [Luke 10:22](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-22.htm); [John 5:26-27](https://biblehub.com/john/5-26.htm); [John 7:16](https://biblehub.com/john/7-16.htm); [John 10:18](https://biblehub.com/john/10-18.htm); [John 12:49-50](https://biblehub.com/john/12-49.htm); [John 17:2](https://biblehub.com/john/17-2.htm)

It is limited by the Father

[Mark 10:40](https://biblehub.com/mark/10-40.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 24:36](https://biblehub.com/matthew/24-36.htm) pp [Mark 13:32](https://biblehub.com/mark/13-32.htm); [Philippians 2:6-8](https://biblehub.com/philippians/2-6.htm)

It is questioned by others

[Matthew 12:24-28](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-24.htm) pp [Mark 3:22-26](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-22.htm) pp [Luke 11:15-20](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-15.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 21:23-27](https://biblehub.com/matthew/21-23.htm) pp [Mark 11:27-33](https://biblehub.com/mark/11-27.htm) pp [Luke 20:1-8](https://biblehub.com/luke/20-1.htm); [John 2:18](https://biblehub.com/john/2-18.htm)

The authority of Jesus Christ is seen in his works

He has authority over nature

[Matthew 8:23-27](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-23.htm) pp [Mark 4:36-41](https://biblehub.com/mark/4-36.htm) pp [Luke 8:22-25](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-22.htm); [Matthew 14:22-33](https://biblehub.com/matthew/14-22.htm) pp [Mark 6:45-51](https://biblehub.com/mark/6-45.htm) pp [John 6:15-21](https://biblehub.com/john/6-15.htm); [Matthew 21:18-22](https://biblehub.com/matthew/21-18.htm) pp [Mark 11:12-14](https://biblehub.com/mark/11-12.htm) pp [Mark 11:20-24](https://biblehub.com/mark/11-20.htm)

He has authority over sin

[Matthew 9:1-8](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-1.htm) pp [Mark 2:3-12](https://biblehub.com/mark/2-3.htm) pp [Luke 5:18-26](https://biblehub.com/luke/5-18.htm); [Luke 7:37-38](https://biblehub.com/luke/7-37.htm)-50

He has authority over sickness

[Matthew 8:14-15](https://biblehub.com/matthew/8-14.htm) pp [Mark 1:30-31](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-30.htm) pp [Luke 4:38-39](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-38.htm); [Matthew 20:29-34](https://biblehub.com/matthew/20-29.htm) pp [Mark 10:46-52](https://biblehub.com/mark/10-46.htm) pp [Luke 18:35-43](https://biblehub.com/luke/18-35.htm); [Acts 3:16](https://biblehub.com/acts/3-16.htm); [Acts 4:30](https://biblehub.com/acts/4-30.htm)

He has authority over evil

[Mark 1:23-27](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-23.htm) pp [Luke 4:33-36](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-33.htm); [Luke 10:17](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-17.htm)

He has authority over death

[Matthew 9:18-25](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-18.htm) pp [Mark 5:22-42](https://biblehub.com/mark/5-22.htm) pp [Luke 8:40-55](https://biblehub.com/luke/8-40.htm); [Matthew 28:1-7](https://biblehub.com/matthew/28-1.htm) pp [Mark 16:1-6](https://biblehub.com/mark/16-1.htm) pp [Luke 24:1-8](https://biblehub.com/luke/24-1.htm) pp [John 20:1-17](https://biblehub.com/john/20-1.htm); [Luke 7:11-16](https://biblehub.com/luke/7-11.htm); [John 11:38-44](https://biblehub.com/john/11-38.htm)

**The authority of Jesus Christ is seen in his teaching**

Jesus Christ claims authority for his own words

[Luke 4:18](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-18.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 5:21-22](https://biblehub.com/matthew/5-21.htm)-28; [Matthew 12:41-42](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-41.htm); [Matthew 17:5](https://biblehub.com/matthew/17-5.htm) pp [Mark 9:7](https://biblehub.com/mark/9-7.htm) pp [Luke 9:35](https://biblehub.com/luke/9-35.htm); [Mark 2:5](https://biblehub.com/mark/2-5.htm); [Mark 8:38](https://biblehub.com/mark/8-38.htm); [Mark 10:15](https://biblehub.com/mark/10-15.htm) The use of the phrase "I tell you the truth" emphasises Jesus Christ's authority; [Mark 13:26](https://biblehub.com/mark/13-26.htm); [John 4:24-26](https://biblehub.com/john/4-24.htm); [John 6:63](https://biblehub.com/john/6-63.htm); [John 7:15-18](https://biblehub.com/john/7-15.htm); [John 12:48-50](https://biblehub.com/john/12-48.htm); [John 13:13](https://biblehub.com/john/13-13.htm); [John 14:6](https://biblehub.com/john/14-6.htm) The "I am" sayings in John's Gospel emphasise the divine nature of Jesus Christ's authority; [John 15:3](https://biblehub.com/john/15-3.htm)

His authority is recognised by others

[Matthew 7:28-29](https://biblehub.com/matthew/7-28.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 22:33](https://biblehub.com/matthew/22-33.htm); [Mark 1:22](https://biblehub.com/mark/1-22.htm) pp [Luke 4:32](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-32.htm)

His authority is questioned and opposed by others

[Matthew 26:65-68](https://biblehub.com/matthew/26-65.htm); [Luke 4:28-30](https://biblehub.com/luke/4-28.htm)
