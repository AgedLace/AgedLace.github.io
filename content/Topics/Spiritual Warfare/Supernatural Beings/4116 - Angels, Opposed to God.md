[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [4000 Creation](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4000.htm)  "  [4100 Supernatural beings](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4100.htm)  "  [4110 angels](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4110.htm)  "  [4116 angels, opposed to God](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/4116.htm)

Spiritual beings who fell with Satan. They are under God's judgment and are subject to the authority of believers.

Spiritual authorities led by Satan

[2 Corinthians 11:14](https://biblehub.com/2_corinthians/11-14.htm) _See also_ [John 12:31](https://biblehub.com/john/12-31.htm); [John 16:11](https://biblehub.com/john/16-11.htm); [Ephesians 6:12](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/6-12.htm); [Revelation 9:11](https://biblehub.com/revelation/9-11.htm)

Angels opposed to God will be judged

Jude 6 _See also_ [Job 4:18](https://biblehub.com/job/4-18.htm); [Job 15:15](https://biblehub.com/job/15-15.htm); [Matthew 25:41](https://biblehub.com/matthew/25-41.htm); [2 Peter 2:4](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/2-4.htm); [Revelation 20:10](https://biblehub.com/revelation/20-10.htm)

Jesus Christ has defeated Satan and his angels

[Colossians 2:15](https://biblehub.com/colossians/2-15.htm) _See also_ [Genesis 3:15](https://biblehub.com/genesis/3-15.htm); [Ephesians 1:19-21](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/1-19.htm)

Satan's angels are resisted by God's faithful angels

[Revelation 12:7-9](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-7.htm) _See also_ [Daniel 10:13](https://biblehub.com/daniel/10-13.htm) The princes of Persia and Greece appear to be spiritual powers who resist God's rule over these regions.

Jesus Christ gives authority over Satan and his angels to believers

[Luke 10:18-19](https://biblehub.com/luke/10-18.htm) _See also_ [Romans 8:38-39](https://biblehub.com/romans/8-38.htm); [Romans 16:20](https://biblehub.com/romans/16-20.htm); [1 Corinthians 6:3](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/6-3.htm); [Ephesians 6:10-13](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/6-10.htm); [James 4:7](https://biblehub.com/james/4-7.htm)
