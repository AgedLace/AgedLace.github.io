[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [3000 Holy Spirit](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/3000.htm)  "  [3200 Holy Spirit, ministry and work of](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/3200.htm)  "  [3203 Holy Spirit, and assurance](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/3203.htm)  "  [3245 Holy Spirit, blasphemy against](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/3245.htm)

The denial of the action of the Holy Spirit in the ministry of Jesus Christ, or the attribution of his works to demonic influence.

Jesus Christ warns against blasphemy

[Mark 3:29](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-29.htm) pp [Matthew 12:31-32](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-31.htm) pp [Luke 12:10](https://biblehub.com/luke/12-10.htm) _See also_ [1 John 5:16](https://biblehub.com/1_john/5-16.htm)

Blasphemy as erroneously attributing the work of the Holy Spirit to demonic influence

Jesus Christ is accused of being possessed by demons

[Mark 3:22](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-22.htm) pp [Matthew 12:24](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-24.htm) pp [Luke 11:15](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-15.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 10:25](https://biblehub.com/matthew/10-25.htm); [John 7:20](https://biblehub.com/john/7-20.htm); [John 8:48](https://biblehub.com/john/8-48.htm); [John 10:20](https://biblehub.com/john/10-20.htm)

Jesus Christ is accused of casting out demons by demonic power

[Matthew 9:34](https://biblehub.com/matthew/9-34.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 12:24](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-24.htm) pp [Mark 3:22](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-22.htm) pp [Luke 11:15](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-15.htm)

Jesus Christ refutes these charges

[Matthew 12:25-29](https://biblehub.com/matthew/12-25.htm) pp [Mark 3:23-27](https://biblehub.com/mark/3-23.htm) pp [Luke 11:17-22](https://biblehub.com/luke/11-17.htm)

Blasphemy as acting in ways which deny the Holy Spirit's work

[Acts 5:3](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-3.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 63:10](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/63-10.htm); [1 Thessalonians 5:19](https://biblehub.com/1_thessalonians/5-19.htm); [Hebrews 10:26-31](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/10-26.htm)
