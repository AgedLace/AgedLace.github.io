- Topical
	- Find every verse and reference that addresses the theme.
	- Build a foundation and conclusion on the topic.
- Character / Place
- Chronological
- Book
	- Context is critical
	- Verse-by-verse, or paragraph-by-paragraph
	- Look for
		- parallels
		- contrasts
- Word
	- Usually done in conjunction with one of the previous studies
	- Also look at where the word is used in a particular passage
