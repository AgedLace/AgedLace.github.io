## **Separate**

[ A-1,Verb,[G873](https://studybible.info/strongs/G873 "G873"), _aphorizo_ ] #Greek/G873-aphorizo\
to mark off by bounds" (apo, "from," horizo, "to determine;" horos, "a limit"), "to separate," is used of "\
(a) the Divine action in setting men apart for the work of the gospel, [Romans 1:1](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Romans%201:1 "Romans 1:1 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"); [Galatians 1:15](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Galatians%201:15 "Galatians 1:15 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV");\
(b) the Divine judgment upon men, [Matthew 13:49](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Matthew%2013:49 "Matthew 13:49 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"); [Matthew 25:32](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Matthew%2025:32 "Matthew 25:32 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV");\
(c) the separation of Christians from unbelievers, [Acts 19:9](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Acts%2019:9 "Acts 19:9 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"); [2 Corinthians 6:17](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/2%20Corinthians%206:17 "2 Corinthians 6:17 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV");\
(d) the separation of believers by unbelievers, [Luke 6:22](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Luke%206:22 "Luke 6:22 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV");\
(e) the withdrawal of Christians from their brethren, [Galatians 2:12](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Galatians%202:12 "Galatians 2:12 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"). In\
(c) is described what the Christian must do, in\
(d) what he must be prepared to suffer, and in\
(e) what he must avoid."* [* From Notes on Galatians, by Hogg and Vine, p. 83.]  
  
[ A-2,Verb,[G5563](https://studybible.info/strongs/G5563 "G5563"), _chorizo_ ] #Greek/G5563-chorizo\
"to put asunder, separate," is translated "to separate" in [Romans 8:35](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Romans%208:35 "Romans 8:35 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"), [Romans 8:39](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Romans%208:39 "Romans 8:39 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"); in the Middle Voice, "to separate oneself, depart" (See [DEPART](https://studybible.info/vines/Depart "Depart")); in the Passive Voice in [Hebrews 7:26](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Hebrews%207:26 "Hebrews 7:26 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"), RV, "separated" (AV, "separate"), the verb here relates to the resurrection of Christ, not, as AV indicates, to the fact of His holiness in the days of His flesh; the list is progressive in this respect that the first three qualities apply to His sinlessness, the next to His resurrection, the last to His ascension. See [PUT](https://studybible.info/vines/Put "Put"), No. 14.  
  
[ A-3,Verb,[G592](https://studybible.info/strongs/G592 "G592"), _apodiorizo_ ] #Greek/G592-apodiorizo\
"to mark off" (apo, "from," dia, "asunder," horizo, "to limit"), hence denotes metaphorically to make "separations," [Jude 1:19](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Jude%201:19 "Jude 1:19 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV"), RV (AV, "separate themselves"), of persons who make divisions (in contrast with [Jude 1:20](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Jude%201:20 "Jude 1:20 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV")); there is no pronoun in the original representing "themselves."  
  
[ B-1,Preposition,[G5565](https://studybible.info/strongs/G5565 "G5565"), _choris_ ] #Greek/G5565-choris\
"apart from, without" (cp. aneu, "without," a rarer word than this), is translated "separate from" in [Ephesians 2:12](https://studybible.info/TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV/Ephesians%202:12 "Ephesians 2:12 TR-KJV_Strongs-ERV") (AV, "without"). See [APART](https://studybible.info/vines/Apart "Apart"), [BESIDE](https://studybible.info/vines/Be "Be"), [WITHOUT](https://studybible.info/vines/Without "Without").
