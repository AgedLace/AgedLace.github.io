See [DEDICATE](https://studybible.info/vines/Dedicate,%20Dedication "Dedicate, Dedication"), [PERFECT](https://studybible.info/vines/Perfect%20(Adjective%20and%20Verb),%20Perfectly "Perfect (Adjective and Verb), Perfectly").
