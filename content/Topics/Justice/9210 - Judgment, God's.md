[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [9000 Last things](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/9000.htm)  "  [9200 Judgment](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/9200.htm)  "  [9210 judgment, God's](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/9210.htm)

God judges the world by identifying and condemning sin and by vindicating and rewarding the righteous. God exercises temporal judgment on the world and on his people; final judgment will take place when Jesus Christ returns.

The nature of God's judgment

Its certainty

[Ecclesiastes 12:14](https://biblehub.com/ecclesiastes/12-14.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 7:11](https://biblehub.com/psalms/7-11.htm); [Ecclesiastes 3:17](https://biblehub.com/ecclesiastes/3-17.htm); [Ecclesiastes 11:9](https://biblehub.com/ecclesiastes/11-9.htm); [James 5:9](https://biblehub.com/james/5-9.htm)

Its righteousness

[Genesis 18:25](https://biblehub.com/genesis/18-25.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 9:7-8](https://biblehub.com/psalms/9-7.htm); [Psalm 50:6](https://biblehub.com/psalms/50-6.htm); [Psalm 96:13](https://biblehub.com/psalms/96-13.htm) pp [1 Chronicles 16:33](https://biblehub.com/1_chronicles/16-33.htm)

Its impartiality

[2 Chronicles 19:7](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/19-7.htm) _See also_ [Deuteronomy 10:17](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/10-17.htm); [Job 34:19](https://biblehub.com/job/34-19.htm); [Psalm 98:9](https://biblehub.com/psalms/98-9.htm); [Romans 2:11](https://biblehub.com/romans/2-11.htm); [Ephesians 6:9](https://biblehub.com/ephesians/6-9.htm)

The purpose of God's judgment

To display his glory

[Isaiah 5:16](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/5-16.htm) _See also_ [Exodus 14:4](https://biblehub.com/exodus/14-4.htm); [Isaiah 59:18-19](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/59-18.htm); [Ezekiel 7:27](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/7-27.htm); [Ezekiel 38:23](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/38-23.htm); [Revelation 14:7](https://biblehub.com/revelation/14-7.htm)

To vindicate the righteous

[1 Samuel 24:12-15](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/24-12.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 7:8-9](https://biblehub.com/psalms/7-8.htm); [Isaiah 34:8](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/34-8.htm); [Jeremiah 11:20](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/11-20.htm); [Jeremiah 51:9-10](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/51-9.htm); [Revelation 16:5-7](https://biblehub.com/revelation/16-5.htm)

To defend the weak

[Psalm 140:12](https://biblehub.com/psalms/140-12.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 82:1-4](https://biblehub.com/psalms/82-1.htm); [Isaiah 11:4](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/11-4.htm); [Ezekiel 34:16-22](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/34-16.htm)

To bring salvation to his people

[Isaiah 30:18](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/30-18.htm) _See also_ [Exodus 6:6](https://biblehub.com/exodus/6-6.htm); [Deuteronomy 32:36](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/32-36.htm); [Psalm 76:8-9](https://biblehub.com/psalms/76-8.htm); [Psalm 105:5-7](https://biblehub.com/psalms/105-5.htm) pp [1 Chronicles 16:12-14](https://biblehub.com/1_chronicles/16-12.htm); [Isaiah 33:22](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/33-22.htm)

To punish sin

[Romans 2:12](https://biblehub.com/romans/2-12.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 1:4-6](https://biblehub.com/psalms/1-4.htm); [John 12:48](https://biblehub.com/john/12-48.htm); [Romans 5:16](https://biblehub.com/romans/5-16.htm); [Hebrews 10:26-30](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/10-26.htm); [Hebrews 13:4](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/13-4.htm)

To turn people to God

[Isaiah 19:22](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/19-22.htm) Temporal judgments may turn people to God and so save them from eternal judgment. _See also_ [1 Kings 8:33](https://biblehub.com/1_kings/8-33.htm) pp [2 Chronicles 6:24](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/6-24.htm); [2 Chronicles 7:13-14](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/7-13.htm); [Daniel 4:33-34](https://biblehub.com/daniel/4-33.htm); [Hosea 2:5-7](https://biblehub.com/hosea/2-5.htm); [1 Corinthians 11:29-32](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/11-29.htm)

God's judgment may be delayed

[Job 24:1-4](https://biblehub.com/job/24-1.htm); [2 Peter 3:9](https://biblehub.com/2_peter/3-9.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 74:10-11](https://biblehub.com/psalms/74-10.htm); [Psalm 94:2-3](https://biblehub.com/psalms/94-2.htm); [Habakkuk 1:2-4](https://biblehub.com/habakkuk/1-2.htm); [Acts 17:30](https://biblehub.com/acts/17-30.htm); [Romans 3:25](https://biblehub.com/romans/3-25.htm); [Revelation 6:10](https://biblehub.com/revelation/6-10.htm)

Examples of God's judgment

Judgment on ungodly individuals

[Acts 12:23](https://biblehub.com/acts/12-23.htm) _See also_ [2 Chronicles 21:18-19](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/21-18.htm) Jehoram; [Daniel 5:26-28](https://biblehub.com/daniel/5-26.htm) Belshazzar; [Revelation 2:22](https://biblehub.com/revelation/2-22.htm) Jezebel, the false prophetess

Judgment following particular sins

[Genesis 4:10-14](https://biblehub.com/genesis/4-10.htm) Cain's murder of Abel; [Numbers 20:12](https://biblehub.com/numbers/20-12.htm) Moses' and Aaron's disobedience; [Leviticus 10:1-2](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/10-1.htm) Nadab's and Abihu's disobedience; [1 Samuel 25:38-39](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/25-38.htm) Nabal's contemptuous treatment of David; [2 Samuel 6:6-8](https://biblehub.com/2_samuel/6-6.htm) Uzzah's handling of the ark; [2 Kings 2:23-24](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/2-23.htm) youths jeering at Elisha; [2 Kings 5:27](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/5-27.htm) Gehazi's greed; [2 Chronicles 26:19-20](https://biblehub.com/2_chronicles/26-19.htm) Uzziah's pride and unfaithfulness; [Acts 5:1-10](https://biblehub.com/acts/5-1.htm) Ananias' and Sapphira's lies

[Joel 3:12-13](https://biblehub.com/joel/3-12.htm) _See also_ [Genesis 6:17](https://biblehub.com/genesis/6-17.htm); [Genesis 19:24-25](https://biblehub.com/genesis/19-24.htm)
