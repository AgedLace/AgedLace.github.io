[Dictionary of Bible Themes](https://biblehub.com/topical/dictionary_of_bible_themes.htm)  "  [5000 Humanity](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5000.htm)  "  [5760 Human attitudes and behaviour](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5760.htm)  "  [5827 curse](https://biblehub.com/topical/dbt/5827.htm)

An expression of contempt or malediction for someone. To be cursed is to suffer various kinds of misfortune, sometimes to the extent of being cut off from one's family or community, or of suffering death itself.

Divine curses

Divine curses came into effect after the fall

[Genesis 3:14-19](https://biblehub.com/genesis/3-14.htm) _See also_ [Genesis 5:29](https://biblehub.com/genesis/5-29.htm); [Genesis 8:21](https://biblehub.com/genesis/8-21.htm) This curse refers to the flood; the curse of Ge 3:17 is not revoked here.

Divine curses come from disobeying the law

[Deuteronomy 28:15-19](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/28-15.htm) _See also_ [Leviticus 26:14-39](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/26-14.htm); [Deuteronomy 11:26-28](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/11-26.htm); [Deuteronomy 27:9-26](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/27-9.htm); [Deuteronomy 28:20-68](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/28-20.htm); [Deuteronomy 29:18-21](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/29-18.htm); [Jeremiah 11:2-4](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/11-2.htm); [Daniel 9:11-14](https://biblehub.com/daniel/9-11.htm); [Galatians 3:10](https://biblehub.com/galatians/3-10.htm)

Human curses

Human curses upon other human beings

[2 Kings 2:23-24](https://biblehub.com/2_kings/2-23.htm) _See also_ [Genesis 9:24-25](https://biblehub.com/genesis/9-24.htm); [Joshua 9:22-23](https://biblehub.com/joshua/9-22.htm); [1 Samuel 14:24-28](https://biblehub.com/1_samuel/14-24.htm); [Nehemiah 13:25](https://biblehub.com/nehemiah/13-25.htm)

Human curses upon God

[Job 2:9](https://biblehub.com/job/2-9.htm); [Revelation 16:9](https://biblehub.com/revelation/16-9.htm) _See also_ [Isaiah 52:5](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/52-5.htm); [Ezekiel 20:27](https://biblehub.com/ezekiel/20-27.htm)

Human beings are forbidden to curse God and others

[Leviticus 24:15-16](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/24-15.htm)

Cursing rulers:

[Exodus 22:28](https://biblehub.com/exodus/22-28.htm); [1 Kings 21:13](https://biblehub.com/1_kings/21-13.htm)

Cursing one's father or mother:

[Exodus 21:17](https://biblehub.com/exodus/21-17.htm); [Matthew 15:4](https://biblehub.com/matthew/15-4.htm) pp [Mark 7:10](https://biblehub.com/mark/7-10.htm); [Leviticus 20:9](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/20-9.htm) [Leviticus 19:14](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/19-14.htm) cursing the deaf

People can curse the day they were born

[Jeremiah 20:14](https://biblehub.com/jeremiah/20-14.htm) _See also_ [Job 3:1-10](https://biblehub.com/job/3-1.htm)

Paul wishes himself cursed for the sake of Israel

[Romans 9:3-4](https://biblehub.com/romans/9-3.htm)

Jesus Christ took the curse of the law upon himself

[Galatians 3:13-14](https://biblehub.com/galatians/3-13.htm) _See also_ [Deuteronomy 21:22-23](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/21-22.htm)

Christians are exhorted to bless those who curse them

[Luke 6:27-28](https://biblehub.com/luke/6-27.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 5:39-42](https://biblehub.com/matthew/5-39.htm) pp [Luke 6:29-30](https://biblehub.com/luke/6-29.htm); [Romans 12:14](https://biblehub.com/romans/12-14.htm); [1 Corinthians 4:12-13](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/4-12.htm)
