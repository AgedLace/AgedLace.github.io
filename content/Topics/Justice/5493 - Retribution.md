Recompense for evil. In Scripture this is the prerogative of God and of the authorities ordained by God, and never of individuals.

The principle of retribution

[Exodus 20:5-6](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-5.htm); [Romans 12:19](https://biblehub.com/romans/12-19.htm); [Hebrews 10:30](https://biblehub.com/hebrews/10-30.htm) _See also_ [Deuteronomy 32:35](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/32-35.htm); [Exodus 32:33-34](https://biblehub.com/exodus/32-33.htm); [Leviticus 26:23-25](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/26-23.htm); [Deuteronomy 7:9-10](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/7-9.htm); [Deuteronomy 32:41-43](https://biblehub.com/deuteronomy/32-41.htm); [Job 34:11](https://biblehub.com/job/34-11.htm); [Proverbs 24:12](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/24-12.htm); [Isaiah 1:24](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/1-24.htm); [Nahum 1:2](https://biblehub.com/nahum/1-2.htm)

Retribution is often built into the nature of wrongdoing

[Psalm 7:15-16](https://biblehub.com/psalms/7-15.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 37:14-15](https://biblehub.com/psalms/37-14.htm); [Proverbs 1:18](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/1-18.htm); [Proverbs 26:27](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/26-27.htm); [Proverbs 28:10](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/28-10.htm); [Ecclesiastes 10:8](https://biblehub.com/ecclesiastes/10-8.htm); [Matthew 26:52](https://biblehub.com/matthew/26-52.htm); [Galatians 6:7-8](https://biblehub.com/galatians/6-7.htm)

Retribution is placed in the hands of governments

[Genesis 9:6](https://biblehub.com/genesis/9-6.htm); [Exodus 21:23-25](https://biblehub.com/exodus/21-23.htm) This "lex talionis" (law of exacting like for like) was not for private retribution. It insisted that justice be fair and not out of proportion to the crime ; [Romans 13:4](https://biblehub.com/romans/13-4.htm) _See also_ [Leviticus 24:17-22](https://biblehub.com/leviticus/24-17.htm); [Numbers 35:16-25](https://biblehub.com/numbers/35-16.htm); [1 Peter 2:14](https://biblehub.com/1_peter/2-14.htm)

Private retribution is forbidden

[Proverbs 24:29](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/24-29.htm) _See also_ [Matthew 5:38-39](https://biblehub.com/matthew/5-38.htm); [Romans 12:19](https://biblehub.com/romans/12-19.htm)

Prayer for retribution

[Psalm 28:4](https://biblehub.com/psalms/28-4.htm) Christians can agree with these prayers, hating all that opposes the Lord and goodness, and gladly anticipating the ultimate vindication of God's glory. Christians leave judgment in God's hands, but nonetheless grieve over the world's pain and over the death of every unrepentant sinner ; [2 Thessalonians 1:6-10](https://biblehub.com/2_thessalonians/1-6.htm) _See also_ [Psalm 79:10](https://biblehub.com/psalms/79-10.htm); [Psalm 94:1-2](https://biblehub.com/psalms/94-1.htm); [Psalm 137:8-9](https://biblehub.com/psalms/137-8.htm); [Lamentations 3:64-66](https://biblehub.com/lamentations/3-64.htm)

National retribution
