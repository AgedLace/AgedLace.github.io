## Zechariah 5:1-4

* **Every thief is removed**
* **Every perjurer is removed**
	* BIB, NKJV - removed = expelled
	* NIV, NLT - banished
	* ESV - cleaned out
	* KJV, AMP, ASV - cut off
	* NASB - purged
	* CSB - has gone unpunished
	* HCSB - will be removed
	* Aramaic Bible in Plain English - according to it he will be overcome
	* LXX - shall be punished with death
	* ISV - will be ejected
	* H5352 naqah
		* to be empty or clean
		* acquit at all, altogether, be blameless, cleanse, be clearing, cut off
		* A primitive root; to be (or make) clean (literally or figuratively); by implication (in an adverse sense) to be bare, *i.e.* Extirpated -- acquit X at all, X altogether, be blameless, cleanse, (be) clear(-ing), cut off, be desolate, be free, be (hold) guiltless, be (hold) innocent, X by no means, be quit, be (leave) unpunished, X utterly, X wholly
		* *be cleaned out*, of a plundered city [Isaiah 3:26](https://biblehub.com/interlinear/isaiah/3-26.htm); *purged out*, of liars and perjurers [Zechariah 5:3](https://biblehub.com/interlinear/zechariah/5-3.htm) (twice in verse).
* God has sent the curse out and it enters the house of the thief and the perjurer
* This curse will remain inside their houses and destroy it, down to its timbers and stones
