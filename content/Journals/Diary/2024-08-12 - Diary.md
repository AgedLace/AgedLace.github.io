## Things God Hates and Detests

- Prov 6:16-19 #OT/Proverbs-6v16-19 #God/Hates/7-Things
	- 6 things the LORD hates; 7 that are detestable to Him
		- haughty eyes #God/Hates/Haughty-Eyes
		- lying tongue #God/Hates/Lying-Tongue
		- hands that shed innocent blood #God/Hates/the-Shedding-of-Innocent-Blood
		- a heart that devises wicked schemes #God/Hates/Hearts-Devising-Wicked-Schemes
		- feet that run swiftly to evil #God/Hates/Feed-That-Run-to-Evil
		- a false witness who gives false testimony #God/Hates/False-Witnesses-and-Testmony
		- one who stirs up discord among brothers #God/Hates/One-Who-Stirs-Up-Discord
- Deuteronomy 7:10 #OT/Deuteronomy-7v10
	- God repays those who hate Him to their faces with destruction; He does not hesitate to repay them to their face #God/Repays/Those-Who-Hate-Him/To-Their-Faces #God/Repays/Those-Who-Hate-Him/With-Destruction

	- If a man hates his neighbor
		- AND LIES IN WAIT and kills him, #Murderer/Lies-in-Wait 
		- then flees to a City of Refuge #City/of-Refuge
		- the elders of the city MUST
			- send for him

## Regarding Pillars of Cloud

#OT/Numbers-10v34-36

> [!NOTE] **Numbers 10:34-36**
> 
> 34 And the cloud of the LORD was over them by day when they set out from the camp. 35 Whenever the ark set out, Moses would say, "Rise up, O LORD! May Your enemies be scattered; may those who hate You flee before You." 36 And when it came to rest, he would say: "Return, O LORD, to the countless thousands of Israel."
