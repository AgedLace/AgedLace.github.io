## 2024-07-10 - Diary

Holy Father,

I'm desiring to create a website, or other means, of putting articles about things You are teaching me for others to see.

I've been down this road before … and this has not worked out well because it has served as distractions to keep me from spending time in Your Word, and because I have no power to draw the audience that would benefit from what You would have me post.  

So, I ask You … do YOU want me to have a website, or other means, of sharing Your Word with others? Do YOU have a means of expanding my sphere of influence to effectively and efficiently bring in a harvest of souls for Your glory and honor?  

I need You to show me either YES or NO … and if YES, then I need You to show me what platform or medium You want used … and where You want it based.  

I don't want any more distractions. I need to doing Your work. But I need to know what You want me to do, and how You want me to do, and how You want me to do it.
