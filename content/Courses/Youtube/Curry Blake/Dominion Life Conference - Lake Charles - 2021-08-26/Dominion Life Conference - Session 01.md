### God's Word is the Final Authority

* [[Psalm 119:89]]
	* *89 Your word, O LORD, [is] everlasting; it is firmly fixed in the heavens.*
		* BSB - "firmly fixed" - [[H5324 - natsab]]
			* 1) to stand, take one's stand, stand upright, be set (over), establish 
			* 1a) (Niphal) 
				* 1a1) to station oneself, take one's stand 
				* 1a2) to stand, be stationed 
				* 1a3) to stand, take an upright position 
				* 1a4) to be stationed, be appointed 
				* 1a5) deputy, prefect, appointed, deputed (subst) 
				* 1a6) to stand firm 
			* 1b) (Hiphil) 
				* 1b1) to station, set 
				* 1b2) to set up, erect 
				* 1b3) to cause to stand erect 
				* 1b4) to fix, establish 
			* 1c) (Hophal) to be fixed, be determined, be stationary
		* KJV - "settled" - to establish as a boundary beyond which you do not go [Curry's definition]

> Our job is to settle (God's Word) in our hearts, settle it in our minds, and settle it on this earth because it's already settled in heaven. - Curry Blake

> You have to make up your mind what you are going to believe. - Curry Blake

> There is a lifestyle that comes with power. - Curry Blake
