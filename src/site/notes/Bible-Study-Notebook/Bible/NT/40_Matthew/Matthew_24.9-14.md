[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-9.htm) Then they will deliver you over to be [[Believers-Persecution \|persecuted and killed]], and [[Believers-Hated \|you will be hated by all nations because of My name]]. [10](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-10.htm) [[At-That-Time \|At that time]] [[The-Falling-Away \|many will fall away and will betray and hate one another]], [11](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-11.htm) and many [[False-Prophets \|false prophets]] will arise and [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Topics/Deception\|deceive many]].
