**Temple Destruction and Other Signs** 
([Mark 13:1–8](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fbsb%2Fmark%2F13.htm); [Luke 21:5–9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fbsb%2Fluke%2F21.htm%235))

[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-1.htm) As [[Jesus_Prophecies \| Jesus]] left the [[Temple-Destroyed \| temple]] and was walking away, His [[Bible-Study-Notebook/People/Disciples/Disciples-Ask-3-Questions\| disciples]] came up to Him to point out its buildings.

[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-2.htm) "Do you see all these things?" He replied. "Truly I tell you, [[Temple-Destroyed \| not one stone here will be left on another; every one will be thrown down]]."

[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-3.htm) While [[Jesus_Prophecies \| Jesus]] was sitting on the [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Places/Mount-of-Olives\| Mount of Olives]], the [[Bible-Study-Notebook/People/Disciples/Disciples-Ask-3-Questions\| disciples]] came to Him privately. "Tell us," they said, "[[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| when will these things happen]], and [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| what will be the sign of Your coming]] and [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| of the end of the age]]?"

[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-4.htm) [[Jesus_Prophecies \| Jesus]] answered, "[[Bible-Study-Notebook/People/Believers/Commanded/See-To-It\| See to it]] [[Deception \| that no one deceives you]]. [5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-5.htm) For [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Topics/False-Christs\| many will come in My name]], claiming, 'I am the Christ,' and [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Topics/Deception\| will deceive many]]. [6](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-6.htm) You will hear of [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| wars and rumors of wars]], but [[Bible-Study-Notebook/People/Believers/Commanded/See-To-It\| see to it]] that you are not alarmed. These things must happen, but [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/The_End\| the end]] is still to come. [7](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-7.htm) [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| Nation will rise against nation]], and [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| kingdom against kingdom]]. There will be [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| famines and earthquakes in various places]]. [8](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fbiblehub.com%2Fmatthew%2F24-8.htm) All these are the [[Bible-Study-Notebook/Events/End Time/Birth-Pains-Signs\| beginning of birth pains]].
